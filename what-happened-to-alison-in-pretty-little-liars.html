What Happened To Alison In Pretty Little Liars?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, February 7, 2024

Introduction to Alison DiLaurentis

Alison DiLaurentis is a central character in the popular television series “Pretty Little Liars,” which aired from 2010 to 2017. The show, based on the book series by Sara Shepard, follows the lives of four high school girls whose clique falls apart after the disappearance of their leader, Alison. The mystery of what happened to Alison drives much of the show’s plot, with twists and turns that kept viewers on the edge of their seats for seven seasons.

The Disappearance of Alison DiLaurentis

Alison’s disappearance is the event that sets the entire series in motion. One night, during a sleepover with her friends Aria Montgomery, Spencer Hastings, Hanna Marin, and Emily Fields, Alison goes missing. Her friends wake up to find her gone without a trace, and the mystery of her disappearance creates a rift between them.

Return of Alison

After several seasons filled with false leads and red herrings, it is revealed that Alison is, in fact, alive. She had been in hiding, fearing for her life after receiving threats from an unknown antagonist known as “A.” Her return to Rosewood, the fictional town where the series is set, brings more questions than answers.

The Truth About That Night

As the series progresses, the events of the night Alison disappeared become clearer. It is revealed that Alison was attacked and buried alive by her mother, Jessica DiLaurentis, who mistook her for someone else. However, Alison was saved by her friend, Mona Vanderwaal, who also becomes the first “A.”

Alison’s Relationships and Manipulations

Alison was known for her manipulative and sometimes cruel behavior. Her relationships with her friends and others were complex, often involving lies and deceit. She had a particularly complicated relationship with her friend and later love interest, Emily Fields.

Alison’s Family Secrets

The DiLaurentis family is full of secrets, many of which revolve around Alison. It is eventually revealed that Alison has a twin sister named Courtney in the book series, while in the TV series, she has a secret brother, Charles, who later transitions to become Charlotte DiLaurentis, also known as CeCe Drake.

Alison as a Victim

Throughout the series, Alison is often portrayed as a victim of circumstances beyond her control. She is targeted by multiple “A”s and is often at the center of the chaos that surrounds the main characters.

Alison’s Role in the “A” Game

Alison’s return to Rosewood brings her into the “A” game, a series of threats and dangerous tasks orchestrated by the mysterious “A.” She becomes both a player and a pawn in this twisted game.

Alison’s Character Development

Over the course of the series, Alison undergoes significant character development. From a mean girl who manipulated those around her, she grows into a more empathetic and caring individual, partly due to the hardships she endures.

Alison’s Legal Troubles

Alison faces legal troubles when she is framed for the murder of Mona Vanderwaal. She is eventually cleared of these charges, but not before spending time in prison and dealing with the fallout from her tarnished reputation.

Alison’s Love Life

Alison’s love life is a tumultuous aspect of her story. She has relationships with several characters, including Emily Fields, with whom she eventually has children via a controversial surrogacy plotline.

Alison’s Role in the Finale

In the series finale, Alison is instrumental in uncovering the final “A” and bringing an end to the game. She also reconciles with her friends and finds some semblance of peace after years of turmoil.

Alison’s Life After “A”

After the reveal of the final “A,” Alison’s life takes on a new direction. She becomes a teacher at Rosewood High School and is shown to be working towards building a stable and happy life for herself and her family.

Alison’s Legacy

Alison’s legacy in “Pretty Little Liars” is complex. She is both a beloved character and a controversial figure, whose actions and experiences have had a profound impact on the show’s narrative and its characters.

FAQs About Alison DiLaurentis

  • Was Alison DiLaurentis really dead?
    No, Alison was not dead. She was in hiding for several seasons before it was revealed that she was alive.
  • Who was Alison’s most significant love interest?
    Alison’s most significant love interest was Emily Fields, with whom she had a complex and evolving relationship.
  • Did Alison have a twin in the TV series?
    No, in the TV series, Alison did not have a twin. This plot point differs from the book series, where she has a twin named Courtney.

Conclusion

Alison DiLaurentis’ journey in “Pretty Little Liars” is a rollercoaster of emotions, mystery, and character growth. From her initial portrayal as the queen bee with a penchant for manipulation, Alison’s character evolves significantly throughout the series. Her disappearance and the subsequent revelation that she was alive all along were pivotal moments that shaped the narrative of the show. Alison’s experiences with her family’s dark secrets, her entanglement in the “A” game, and her various relationships added depth to her character and kept viewers invested in her story. In the end, Alison emerges as a survivor who strives to overcome her past and build a better future. Her legacy as a character in “Pretty Little Liars” is marked by her resilience, her capacity for change, and the indelible impact she had on the lives of those around her.
