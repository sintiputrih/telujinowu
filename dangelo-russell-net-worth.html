D’Angelo Russell Net Worth: How Much Is D’Angelo Russell Worth? – D’Angelo Russell is a professional basketball player for the Los Angeles Lakers in the NBA. He was born on February 23, 1996, and grew up in Louisville, Kentucky, where he attended Central High School as a freshman in 2010-11.

In 2014, Russell was selected as a McDonald’s All-American and played in the McDonald’s All-American Game and Jordan Brand Classic. He committed to Ohio State on June 7, 2013, choosing the Buckeyes over Louisville, Michigan State, and North Carolina. Russell was a five-star recruit and played one season of college basketball for the Ohio State Buckeyes.

During his freshman year, Russell set several records and received numerous awards. On April 22, 2015, Russell declared for the NBA draft, forgoing his final three years of college eligibility. He was selected by the Los Angeles Lakers with the second overall pick in the 2015 NBA draft. However, on February 6, 2020, Russell was traded to the Minnesota Timberwolves along with Jacob Evans and Omari Spellman for Andrew Wiggins, a protected future first-round draft pick, and a future second-round draft pick.
