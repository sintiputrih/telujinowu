Deborra Lee Furness Married Life With Hugh Jackman, Net Worth, &amp; Weight Loss Process

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, June 20, 2024

People might acknowledge Hugh Jackman more than his wife, Deborah Lee Furness. But the Wolverine actor was too young when Furness already had reached the limelight through her acting career. They enclose a great bond despite their age difference of thirteen years; however, Jackman was interested in Deborah Lee Furness when he first laid his eyes on her.

Deborra Lee Furness and Hugh Jackman (©: Hello Magazine)

They are now married for twenty-four years, nearly a couple and a half-decade. Speaking about their long-term married life, Furness talked with Anh's Brush With Fame and mentioned, 'We just had this amazing connection. And I feel blessed that I experienced that I feel like I met my soulmate, whatever that is.'

Similarly, the Greatest Showman actor has nothing to define as he has always been down to the earth professionally as well as in his personal life. He cites his wife 'soulmate' and is a great disciple of the 'Great Wife, Great Life' motto.

Deborah Lee Furness and Hugh Jackman during the initial phase of their relationship (©: E! Online)

Deborah Lee Furness, age 64, first met in 1995 on the Australian TV series Correlli. In the 10-episode drama, Hugh Jackman portrayed a prisoner who suffered trauma while shifting to jail. And Deborah played the role of a psychologist who tried to rewind his memory.

They dated for a year before they exchanged their wedding vows on 11 April 1996 and have been together since then. Similarly, in the long run of their married life, they got blessed with a couple of children, namely, Oscar Maximillian Jackman and Ava Eliot Jackman. Furness and Jackman adopted Oscar in May 2000. He was born on 15 May 2000. 

Similarly, they adopted a daughter named Ava Eliot Jackman in July 2005. She was born on 10 July 2005.

Deborah Lee Furness' Net Worth

Deborah Lee Furness has a net worth of $50 million, which she derives from her multi-dimensional career as an actress, producer, and director. She has been involved in the entertainment industry for more than four and a half-decades throughout the years. She landed her first role in the 1974's television series The Box. Similarly, she appeared on Division 4, Prisoner: Cell Block H, and Cop Shop until the 80s.

Besides that, she appeared in several movies and tv shows, including Neighbours (1985), Correlli (1995), The Real Macaw (1998), Legend of the Guardians: The Owls of Ga'Hoole (2010), Hyde & Seek (2016), and many more.

Hugh Jackman Annoys In Debora Lee Furness' Weight Loss Process

Deborah Lee, who stands at the height of 5 feet and 8 inches tall, has been following a strict diet regimen along with the physical workouts to go through the weight loss process. She has been going under a keto diet while living in quarantine with her husband, Hugh Jackman. 

It seems funny but is annoying to her as her spouse has picked up a new hobby during this time. Hugh Jackman's quarantine hobby is 'baking bread.' Furness spoke to Studio 10 and mentioned that 'It is so annoying' to find Jackman doing so. She added, 'I'm on a keto diet, which is no carbs.'

Ketogenic diets, which are best known as the keto diet, emphasize cutting carbs and replacing them with healthy high-fat foods. It improves health as well as aids in losing bodyweight. The ingredients of keto diets include grains, starches, and wheat-based products like bread.
