Ariana Grande Shares an Adorable Throwback Photo for Her 28th Birthday

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, June 22, 2024

Ariana Grande celebrated her 28th birthday by looking back at her beginning.

The pop star shared an adorable baby photo of herself on her birthday Instagram post yesterday. The pic shows a closeup of baby Grande's wet face as she's presumably taking a bath.

She captioned the pic, "hbd tiny, I am taking care of you!"

Many of Grande's famous friends commented their well wishes under the post, including John Legend, Scooter Braun, Donatella Versace, and more.

Grande thanked everyone in an Instagram story that read, "Too many beautiful things to repost I am bad at this these days but I feel so deeply grateful & loved thank you so much."

Her husband Dalton Gomez also posted her baby pic in a sweet Tweet that reads, "Happy birthday my dear, all I want to give you is love, care, honesty to each other."

Grande and Gomez were married in an intimate ceremony last May, and the pair have been privately enjoying their newlywed life ever since. A source said earlier this month that the pair felt more relaxed after their marriage.

"Ariana and Dalton felt like before they were married they couldn't be as open about their relationship without it being scrutinized in the media and now they feel way more at ease," they said.

Gomez also posted a wedding photo of the couple (and one of their adorable pups) this morning.
