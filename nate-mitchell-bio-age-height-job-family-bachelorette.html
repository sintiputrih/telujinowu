Meet Bachelorette's Nate Mitchell! His Bio, Age, Height, Job, Family

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, April 17, 2024

Nate Mitchell, full name Nathaniel Mitchell, is one of the contestants in the nineteenth season of ABC’s The Bachelorette. This is the first time in Bachelor’s history that there are two female leads. So, Nate and the other 31 men will be competing for the hearts and roses of two women: former Bachelor contestants Gabby Windey and Rachel Recchia.

The returning series premiered on 11 July 2022 and we are here to tell you all about Nate Mitchell who is full of packages, humble, and hardworking. He says on the show that he is looking for a woman who is kind, adventurous, smart, and ready to complete his beautiful family. So, will Gabby or Rachel wind up going home with him engaged? Let’s find out in this writing called ‘Nate Mitchell Bio.’

Nate Mitchell On The Bachelorette

Amid trying to live life to the fullest and determined to take chance, Nate Mitchell found himself competing for love on The Bachelorette.

Viewers soon got to know a lot about him, that he has a weakness for a woman who can make him laugh so hard his belly hurts, and that he loves to do thoughtful things for the woman he loves, among other things.

However, based on rumors of recent spoilers, Nate does not make it to the final four. Reality Steve took to Twitter to reveal the ladies’ final four and none of them was Nate. According to Steve, Rachel’s top picks include Aven Jones, Tyler Norris, Zach Shallcross, and Tino Franco. And before that, the spoiler king also gave the names of Gaby’s men, Erich Schwer, Jason Alabaster, and Johnny DePhillipo. Justin Budfuloski was also rumored to be Gaby’s fourth.

Nate, however, had not gone home until S19E02 of The Bachelorette. And during that very episode, Nate really starts to open up about his daughter. (Yes! He is a father.)

Nate is shown crying and telling the cameras about how much he misses her, while on a date with Gabby. He gushes about his commitment to his six-year-old daughter and his love of being a girl dad. He describes his little girl as the human form of coffee before adding that she is just like him.

Gabby, on the other hand, while definitely moved by Nate’s fatherly love, also confessed that she is unsure about becoming a mother so soon. Yet, that did not stop Gabby from giving him a rose on a date. She meant when she said that she is still eager to learn about his family and especially his daughter.

Nate Mitchell Job

According to his LinkedIn, Nate Mitchell has been working as an electrical engineer for nearly a decade.

Until January of 2013, Nate was working as a graduate assistant at Southern Illinois University, which required him to teach electrical engineering fundamentals. Then after, he began working as an electrical analyst for Sargent & Lundy, a Chicago-based company that provides comprehensive energy and engineering consultation for new and operating power plants. He continued to be employed there (as of the time of this writing).

At SIU, Nate studied M.S. in Electrical Engineering (2011-2012) and a B.S. in Electrical and Electronics Engineering (2009-2011). He originally attended SIC to play basketball. He thus played the game for Coach Chris Lowery at SIU Carbondale all along doing the academia.
