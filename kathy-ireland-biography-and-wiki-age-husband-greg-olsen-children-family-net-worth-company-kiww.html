Kathy Ireland Biography And Wiki

Kathy Ireland (Kathleen Marie Ireland) is a former American model and actress who converted into authoring and entrepreneurship. She was a supermodel in the 80s and 90s. She is best known for appearing in 13 consecutive Sports Illustrated swimsuit issues.

She was raised in Santa Barbara, California where she did some odd jobs as a child, like selling colored stones and distributing papers in the morning. This would become her building and stepping stone to her career as a businesswoman. Her interest in modeling began when she was in high school. Her mesmerizing body and attitude only helped her become a model in the Elite Modelling Agency who selected her at her school.

She founded a brand marketing company in 1993 called Kathy Ireland Worldwide (kiWW). KiWW has made her one of the wealthiest former models in the world. She had made $420 million personal fortune by 2015, as a businesswoman. She is greatly involved with various charity and non-profit organizations dedicated to education, emergency response and training for children, disease research and management, and HIV/AIDS.

Kathy Ireland Age

Kathy was born on 20th March 1963 in Glendale, California, United States. She is 56 years old as of 2019. Currently, she resides in Santa Barbara, California, U.S. She is likewise a strong Christian and has raised her children in faith and religion.

Kathy Ireland Family

Ireland was born in Glendale, California, as the daughter of John and Barbara Ireland. She has two sisters, Cynthia Ireland, and Mary Ireland. For her education, she graduated from San Marcos High School near Santa Barbara, California.

Kathy Ireland’s Husband: Greg Olsen

Ireland got married to a physician, Greg Olsen, on 20 August in 1988. Together, they have three children: Erik, Lily, and Chloe. Currently, she lives in Southern California with her husband, an emergency room physician and their children. She enjoys being a full-time mother and continues to be a Sunday school teacher as well.

Kathy Ireland Career

 Modeling

Her career in modeling kick-started in 1979, at the age of sixteen when she was scouted by Elite Model Management. She has also featured on covers for Vogue, Forbes, Cosmopolitan, and Mademoiselle.

During the 50th Anniversary event for Sports Illustrated swimsuit, Ireland’s 1989 cover was awarded “The Greatest Sports Illustrated Swimsuit Cover Of All Time” by its publisher.

Acting

Besides modeling, she has likewise appeared in a number of television and movie roles. Her first movie was the 1988’s Alien from L.A., which was later skewered during the fifth season of Mystery Science Theater 3000.

Ireland’s other film appearances include Boy Meets World, Melrose Place, Mom, and Dad Save the World, Side Out, The Larry Sanders Show, Muppets Tonight, Necessary Roughness, Mr. Destiny, National Lampoon’s Loaded Weapon 1 (as Miss. Destiny Demeanor), Once Upon a Christmas, and its sequel Twice Upon a Christmas.

Additionally, she was a participant in the ninth season of Dancing with the Stars, where she and her partner Tony Dovolani were the third couple eliminated in the second week of competition.

Ireland made an appearance in the sixth episode of the fourth season of The Profit in 2016, offering advice for the CEO of Murchison-Hume, Max Kater.

Kathy Ireland Worldwide (KiWW)| Kathy Ireland Company

In 1993, Kathy Ireland Worldwide was founded as a brand marketing firm in an exclusive business relationship with Kmart. Kathy Ireland Worldwide (kiWW) grew to become one of the world’s most valuable global licensors after cutting its ties to the department store chain in 2003. It has consistently ranked among the top 50 licensors, most recently standing at No. 26 on License Global’s “Top 150 Global Licensors” 2019 list with $2.6 billion in retail sales.

It has expanded into many different categories of products, including Fashion Apparel, Wedding, Home, Fine Jewelry, Children’s Toys, Baby, and books, Publishing, Crafts, Gifts and Pet Care.
