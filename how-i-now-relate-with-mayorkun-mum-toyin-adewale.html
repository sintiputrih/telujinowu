How I Now Relate With Mayorkun Mum, Toyin Adewale

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, September 13, 2024

Veteran Nollywood actress Toyin Adewale who doubles as the mother of singer, Mayorkun has described her relationship with the singer since he decided to venture into secular music.

City People caught up with Toyin Adewale for an interview where she disclosed her relationship with her son after his new found music genre away from the gospel music he used to perform in the church.

She said: “It makes me proud and I am happy about it, I never knew Mayowa will go into music. He used to be a junior choir master while in the choir, but never knew he would go this far, because he has always been a very quiet, easy-going person. He is not a loud guy, but you know God’s ways are not our ways, so when he started, I became very happy and proud of him.”
