Hali Okeowo Job

Hali Okeowo got her start as a model when she was very young. She made the decision to launch her professional career even before receiving her bachelor’s degree. She joined the Creative Faces Model Development Agency in 2009 and started collaborating with designers and musicians.

Hali rapidly picked up the business fundamentals after being exposed to high fashion, editorial shoots, and other facets of the art. She joined State Management after graduating, and the modeling agency has subsequently hired her for a variety of tasks. She has also gone outside the box and undertaken various initiatives on her own.

The expert has collaborated with companies like Covergirl and even appeared on “The Jimmy Fallon Show” alongside rapper Big Sean. Hali uses her diverse talents and years of experience in front of the camera to create the most iconic looks for designers, photographers, and others. Her capacity to bring the ideas of the creative team to life is one of the main factors contributing to her steady growth and success. She has successfully presented a vision through her modeling, maintaining consistency in a cutthroat field.

Hali has also embraced diversity in her art. The reality star wore the ornamented and beautifully crafted traditional clothing worn by the Indian population during the celebration of Diwali for a campaign with SEEMA, a beauty, cuisine, travel, and lifestyle platform. Hali, who was featured on the cover of the magazine’s monthly issue, said of the endeavor, “It made me want to learn more about Indian culture. You see the Bollywood movies and how beautiful the girls look. That day, I really felt like that.”

Per her LinkedIn, Hali earned her Bachelor’s degree in broadcast journalism from Penn State University.
