Get To Know Nam Laks Family, Net Worth, Age, Bling Empire

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, April 30, 2024

Nam Laks on Bling Empire: New York was depicted as a spoiled rich kid. She splurged her daddy’s money without a second thought and because of this, she was at risk of being cut off financially!

Keep reading this Nam Laks Bio to learn more about her.

Nam Laks On Bling Empire New York

Netflix’s Bling Empire spinoff Bling Empire: New York premiered on January 20, 2023, and like the original, the show followed a group of affluent Asian Americans residing living their extravagant lifestyles in the city of NY. But whilst almost all of them had made something of themselves, our star, Nam Laks was still depending on her dad to get whatever she wants.

Yet she was affectionately referred to as “The Blair Waldorf of Thailand.”

However, her extravagant lifestyle might soon be coming to an end as her father was thinking of cutting her off upon graduation if she didn’t stand on her own two feet in New York — the other alternative was to return to Thailand.

For those who missed the drama  Nam’s decision to shop for dresses even though she didn’t necessarily “needed” them, was the last straw for her father. As featured in ep 2, she and Vika Abbyaeva go shopping for dresses during a break between her university classes. There, she tells Vika, “It’s fine, my daddy will pay,” when it came to the bill. But after the shopping spree, Nam’s brother told her “After graduation, Dad said you’re on your own, Nam”  and also added that their father would “cancel her card” as she “spends too much.”

How Old Is Nam Laks?

Nam Laks was 28 years of age when she appeared on Bling Empire: New York in 2023.

She was the second younger cast that season — the youngest being Vika Abby (27 then).

Nam Laks Family

Nam Laks comes her an affluent Thai family. Even the star said that her family was “powerful.”

Her father, Nakorn Laksanakarn is a Thai businessman who was the Chief Executive Officer of Natural Park PCL (NPARK). The company in Bangkok was later renamed U City Public Company in 2015. Find him on LinkedIn @nakorn-laksanakarn, IG @nakornla and FB @nakorn.laksanakarn.

Talking about her siblings, she has two brothers Nick and Nino. The Laks siblings share a tight-knit bond.

Her bother, Nino turned 26 on August 10, 2022. He is a Bloxham School and International School Bangkok (ISB) graduate. Find him on IG @ninolaks and FB @nino.laksanakarn

As for Nick, he is a Imperial College London, Royal School of Mines, and The King’s School (Canterbury) graduate. Find him on FB @nick.laksanakarn.

Also, Nam has an aunt named Kingkarn Laksanakarn. She attended Le Cordon Bleu Paris, Regent’s University London, and Stover School. Here’s her FB @kingkarn.laksanakarn.

Nam Laks Net Worth

Fans of Bling Empire know that Nam Laks relied on her father’s credit card. So, it’s hard to say what the star is actually worth. However, if we were to look at the Laks family as one, their net worth is probably was the $100 million range in 2023.

As for Nam’s education, she attended International School Bangkok, Harrow International School, and Woldingham School, and later got her Bachelor’s degree from the University of Manchester and a Master’s degree from Teachers College, Columbia University.

According to Nam, the only reason she got her Master’s was that this was one of her dad’s biggest dreams. “Like, he just loves when his children graduate,” she explained. Also, her dad wanted to graduate from NY to help her step out of her sheltered upbringing and her comfort zone.

Nevertheless, Nam’s newfound independence wasn’t financial in any way. She didn’t control her extravagant spending habits, nor did she take on any part-time job. Infact, she even hired a personal stylist who dressed her for school and events.

“I am definitely daddy’s little girl, but my dad doesn’t always spoil me. There’s a whole list of how to get daddy to say yes,” she said.

Is Nam Laks Dating Anyone?

Nam Laks wasn’t public about her dating life. But considering her IG posts where she was likely having romantic intimate dinners (always with loads of flowers), she might have a boyfriend. And it’s not Kristoff.

Though Nam confessed that she liked Kristoff as he was a “really nice guy,” she friend-zoned him before they could go any further!

Looking back, Nam first met Kristoff at a bar in the Bahamas after Lynn Ban practically pushed the charmer towards Nam. The two chatted for a while and later, Kristoff even paid for Nam’s flight back.

Thereafter, the duo kept in touch via texts, as friends.

Nam Laks Height

Mentioning her district features, Nam has brown hair and eyes, an oval face, and a perfect body.
