Who is Marcos Giron: Biography,Net Worth &amp; more

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, July 9, 2024

Marcos Giron is an American professional tennis player. As per records and statistics, he achieved a career-high singles ranking of No. 49 on 16 May 2022 and a doubles ranking of No. 194 on August 1, 2022. Since he turned professional in 2014, he has been gradually working his way up the ladder but has not really cemented his place among the very best. Prior to becoming a pro, Giron won the boys’ singles in the Ojai Tennis Tournament in 2009 and the men’s invitational in 2011. Apart from that, he won the singles title at the 2014 NCAA Division I Tennis Championships for UCLA.

Who is Marcos Giron?

Marcos Andres Giron was born on July 24, 1993 in Thousand Oaks, California, United States to Andres Giron and Rosanna Giron, both of whom are Spanish. He has a sister Gabriela Giron whom he grew up with. He is an American national and a professional tennis player.

He began playing tennis at a young age of six when he was fed balls by his mother at a local park. His idols in the game are Pete Sampras and Roger Federer. Giron has however not been able to reach the heights of his idols.

After high school, he enrolled at the University of California Los Angeles (UCLA), where he continued with the sport. Over the course of his time there, Giron made many in-roads and achieved great success when he won the the 2014 NCAA Division I Tennis Championships for UCLA.

Giron competes internationally in the Australian Open, French Open, Wimbledon and the US Open. He is ranked No. 69 by the ATP Rankings and achieved a career-high singles ranking of No. 49, and on August 1, 2022, he reached a doubles ranking of No. 194.
