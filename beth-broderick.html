    Beth Broderick Facts

  • Growing up, Beth was a complete nerd and had a keen interest in theatre.
  • She helped co-found New York’s very first AIDS organization called Momentum. Other than that, she is a volunteer for Hollygrove: Children & Family Services as well as the Celebrity Action Council of the City Light Women’s Rehabilitation Program at the Los Angeles Mission.
  • Beth made her debut theatrical appearance as Ella in the film In Love in 1983. She was cast alongside the likes of actor Jerry Butler, Kelly Nichols, and Jack Wrangler.
  • In her free time, she likes to cook and bake. Aside from that, Beth also loves flower arranging, reading, and writing.
  • Beth founded the production company Regardless Films in the past.
  • She and actor Matthew Broderick do not share any kind of relationship with one another.
  • After completing her acting studies at the American Academy of Arts, Beth moved to New York City in an effort to build a steady acting career.
  • Her acting credits also include Man of the Year, Maternal Instincts, Breast Men, Psycho Beach Party, The Inner Circle,  Married… with Children, The 5 Mrs. Buchanans, Hearts Afire, Supernatural, and Glory Days.
  • Dennis Bailey is her writing partner.
