Jacob Elordi and Kaia Jordan Gerber Have a PDA-Filled Date Night

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, January 31, 2024
  • Euphoria star Jacob Elordi and model Kaia Jordan Gerber seem to have confirmed recent dating rumors after the two were spotted on a PDA-filled night out in New York City earlier this week.
  • Elordi was last linked to his costar Zendaya, while Gerber was last known to have dated Saturday Night Live comedian Pete Davidson.

Despite recent reports denying any romantic affiliation between the two, Euphoria actor Jacob Elordi and model Kaia Jordan Gerber were recently spotted showing off some PDA during a night out in New York City.

The two held hands as they strolled the streets. Gerber wore jeans, a black crop top, and a leather blazer, while Elordi also went casual with a floral Hawaiian button-up tucked into cuffed denim. Other photos show Elordi wrapping his arm around Gerber's shoulders.

TheImageDirect.com

The couple was also recently spotted grabbing dinner at celebrity-favorite Nobu in Malibu. Reports at the time alleged that the two were just friends, although their relationship occasionally bordered on flirtatious.

"Jacob definitely has interest in dating Kaia, but there is nothing serious going on there," a source told E! News. "They have many mutual friends and have hung out many times in the past. They both have very chill personalities and similar interests. Jacob makes Kaia laugh and her family adores him."

Meanwhile, the insider added that Gerber, who was last publicly linked to Pete Davidson, isn't looking for anything serious. "Kaia is keeping things friendly for now but there is flirtation between the two," the source said. "She has told friends she is single and is only having fun and hanging out with people."

Elordi was last known to be dating his Euphoria costar Zendaya.
