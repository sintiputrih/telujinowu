Michelle Phan Biography

Michelle Phan is an American makeup artist, entrepreneur, and voice actress who is known as a pioneering Beauty YouTuber, with over 8.9 million subscribers, 1.1 billion lifetime views, and 385 uploaded videos.

In 2005, Phan had a personal blog in which she discussed various makeup tutorials and received requests for further instruction. Under the username Ricebunny, she started posting tutorial vlogs on Xanga and then began publishing on YouTube in May 2007.

BuzzFeed featured two of Phan’s 2009 and 2010 makeup tutorials, “How To Get Lady Gaga ‘s Eyes,” which helped them go viral and brought her over a million subscribers. In 2010, after featuring some of their products in her videos, Lancôme made Phan their official video make-up artist, making her the first Vietnamese spokesperson at the company.

In 2011, Phan co-founded MyGlam, a monthly beauty product subscription service that has since been renamed Ipsy, introduced in September 2012; Ipsy is a supporter of the Generation Beauty Conference. In 2012, Phan became a YouTube advertisement partner and introduced FAWN, a multi-channel YouTube MCN (MCN).

Michelle Phan Education

Phan moved to Tampa, Florida with her family where she attended Tampa Bay Technical High School. Phan attended Ringling College of Art and Design but was discontinued due to financial difficulties. Phan received her Honorary Doctorate of Arts from Ringling in March 2014.

Michelle Phan Family

According to Phan, when she was young her biological father left the family and her mother remarried but left Phan’s step-father after years of abuse. Their mother was followed by Phan, her brother, and half-sister.

Michelle Phan Husband

Phan has been with the dancer and model Dominique Capraro since 2010, in a long-term relationship. The two began in a long-distance relationship and Capraro was featured in a couple of Phan ‘s videos. In an interview, Phan revealed that she no longer posted Capraro ‘s videos or photos, and wanted to keep her relationship with him private.

Michelle Phan Net Worth

Michelle Phan Em Cosmetics

On 15 August 2013, L’Oreal launched Michelle Phan’s latest cosmetic line called EM Cosmetics, dedicating the brand to her mother. Phan acquired L’Oreal ‘s share of EMC Cosmetics through Ipsy in April 2015 and confirmed it on her Instagram in December 2016; she later acquired the company from IPSY and re-launched it in April 2017. Phan also resigned from Ipsy during this time in order to fill in the company.

Michelle Phan Copyright infringement lawsuit

In July 2014, Ultra Records sued Phan for copyright infringement of the songs used in her YouTube videos at the United States District Court for Los Angeles. Ultra demanded damages of up to $150,000 per violation for up to 50 suspected violation incidents, for a total of up to $7.5 million.

Representatives of Phan confirmed that she had obtained permission from Ultra to use the recording, and on September 18, 2014, Phan filed a countersuit against Ultra Records at the Central District of California United States District Court. Both suit and countersuit were dropped in August 2015, when both sides decided to settle out of court. The terms of the contract have not been made public.
