Kim Kardashian Wore a Nude Bodysuit With Crimped Hair

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, June 1, 2024

Queen of beauty and shapewear Kim Kardashian is proving once again that she can pull off any hairstyle. The 41-year-old businesswoman starred in her company SKIMS's latest campaign, where she rocked a skintight bodysuit and '80s-styled hair.

The founder appeared in a new ad wearing a tight, turtleneck catsuit, which she paired with long teased and crimped caramel tresses. She paired her jumper with a beige trench coat that hung off one shoulder and black pointy-toe pumps. The social media star was joined by other women modeling similar outfits in other colors and cuts.

The images, taken by Vanessa Beecroft, are promoting the brand's newest collection, All-In-One. It's a line of "total body-sculpting shape wear" in sizes XXS-4X, available on skims.com starting today. In another campaign photo, Kim is seen rocking a navy blue, long-sleeve bike short onesie. All-In-One also includes strapless and one-shoulder bodysuits, among other selections.

The latest drop for the fashion mogul's brand comes after a weekend in New York City with her new beau, Pete Davidson. The two caught a movie and grabbed dinner on Pete's native Staten Island on Saturday. On Monday, the SNL star was seen leaving Kim's hotel in Tribeca after allegedly staying the night. In photos obtained by Daily Mail, the comedian was all smiles as he exited the Four Seasons alone in a printed sherpa jacket while smoking a joint.
