Kate Upton Acting - The Other Woman Movie Trailer

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, August 31, 2024

Hellin Kay

Photo: Courtesy of YouTube

The trailer for The Other Woman just hit the Internet, and it's worth watching, not just because you're bored at work or hungover from holiday parties (but aren't you?) but also because Kate Upton's in it. It's the Sports Illustrated and ELLE cover star's first major acting role, and people are gonna be talking—about her boobs, obviously—but about her acting chops.

Related: Kate Upton's September 2013 Cover Shoot

Inevitably, haters are gonna throw shade on her performance, even though she only says, like, two sentences in the trailer. It's just a common thing we do, tearing down a model for trying to crossover into acting (see: Brooklyn Decker making the leap in 2011's Just Go With It.) And it's kind of shitty of us. Yes, Kate seems like she can hold her own here— even next to really, REALLY funny ladies Cameron Diaz and Leslie Mann— but if she didn't? I wouldn't be the first to throw stones. Good on her for wanting to expand her career and try out a new skill set. I'll wait til April 25, 2014 (the release date) to judge any further.
