Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, September 2, 2024
Jerry Springer was born on February 13, 1944 in Highgate, England and is best known for hosting the outrageous TV show The Jerry Springer Show. The show gained notoriety for its wild moments, such as onstage fights and scandalous behavior, and made Springer a household name. He was also the former mayor of Cincinnati.
