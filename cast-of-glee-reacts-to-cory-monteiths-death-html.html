The Cast of Glee Reacts to Cory Monteiths Death

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, June 14, 2024

Cory Monteith — also known as the jock turned performer and clumsy dancer with a big voice Finn Hudson on Fox’s Glee — unexpectedly passed away Saturday night at the age of 31. A flood of tributes are certainly to come, but for now we only have the initial tweets and comments from Monteith’s fellow cast members and co-stars. Here are a few words from his colleagues accompanied by samples of the fine work they did together.

Harry Shum Jr., who plays fellow Glee Club member Mike Chang:

At this time, please send nothing but love and light. This tragic news still doesn't seem real to me. I love you Cory.

— Harry Shum Jr (@HarryShumJr) July 14, 2013

Mark Salling, who plays Finn Hudson’s BFF Noah Puckerman:

going through a million memories and emotions today. ill love you always Brother Cory.

— Mark Salling (@MarkSalling) July 14, 2013

Matthew Morrison, who plays teacher Mr. Schue, head of the Glee Club:

Naya Rivera, who plays classmate Santana Lopez:

Dot-Marie Jones, who plays football coach Shannon Beiste:

I AM BLESSED TO HAVE WORKED WITH HIM AND LOVE HIM SO MUCH! MY HEART IS WITH HID FAMILY AND OUR WHOLE GLEE FAMILY! I LOVE YOU ALL!

— Dot-Marie Jones (@dotmariejones) July 14, 2013

Guest stars Idina Menzel (as Rachel Berry’s birth mom, Shelby Corcoran), John Stamos (as Emma’s dentist and ex-husband, Carl Howell), Kristin Chenoweth (as successful William McKinley High School alum April Rhodes), Ricky Martin (as Spanish teacher David Martinez), Gwyneth Paltrow (as substitute teacher Holly Holliday), and Olivia Newton-John and Josh Groban (as themselves):
