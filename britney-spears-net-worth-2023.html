Britney Spears Career

On January 12, 1999, Spears released her debut album “Baby… One More Time.”

It debuted at the top of the Billboard 200 and was certified platinum twice by the Recording Industry Association of America. Her debut album sold over 10 million copies in a year, making it the best-selling album by a teenage artist.

In May 2000, her second album, Oops!… I Did It Again, was released. The album went to number one in the United States and sold over 1.3 million copies. This result is a world record for the highest debut sales by a solo artist.

In February 2001, she signed a 7-8 million dollar deal with Pepsi and released A Mother’s Gift, a book co-written by her own mother. Britney’s third album was released the same year.
