Kylie Jenner's Ex Travis Scott Seems to Shade Timothe Chalamet in New Song &quot;Meltdown&quot;

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, August 14, 2024

Kylie Jenner's ex-boyfriend Travis Scott released a new song today, "Meltdown," and in it, he seems to allude to the cosmetics mogul's budding romance with actor Timothée Chalamet.

In the track, Scott raps, “Chocolate AP and chocolate the Vs (Vs), got the Willy Wonka factory (Vs) / Burn a athlete like it’s calories, find another flame hot as me, bitch.” (Read the full lyrics here.)

“AP” refers to Audemars Piguet's luxury watches, while the Willy Wonka factory mention may be a jab at Chalamet, who stars as the famous fictional chocolatier in the upcoming Wonka film.

Scott and Jenner—who share daughter Stormi, 5, and son Aire Webster, 1—split some months ago, around the holidays, after dating on and off since 2017. Weeks later, in January, Jenner met Chalamet at the Jean Paul Gaultier fashion show during Paris Couture Week. A video of them laughing together at the show was published by celebrity gossip Instagram account Deuxmoi. They also both attended Vanity Fair’s 2023 Oscars after-party in March, but were not seen or photographed together.

By April, rumors that the two were secretly dating began circulating. These were only heightened when one of Jenner’s cars was photographed parked outside of Chalamet’s Beverly Hills home. Then, this past June, Jenner and Chalamet were photographed together for the first time at the actor’s house. In photos published by the Daily Mail from two separate days, the pair is seen walking side by side while holding hot drinks.

Neither party has publicly confirmed the relationship.
