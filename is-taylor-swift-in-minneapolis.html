Getting to the Concert Venue

Getting to U.S. Bank Stadium, where the Taylor Swift concert will be held, is convenient due to the compact downtown area. There are various transportation options available, including public transit, rideshare services, and parking options.

Public Transportation Options

Public transportation provides an efficient and hassle-free way to reach the Taylor Swift concert at U.S. Bank Stadium. The stadium is easily accessible via bus routes and the light rail, which stops right in front of the venue. Whether you’re coming from the north, south, or east/west, there are bus routes available to transport you to the concert. The METRO Green Line connects downtown St. Paul to downtown Minneapolis, while the METRO Blue Line connects Minneapolis to the MSP Airport, both of which stop at U.S. Bank Stadium. For more information about bus routes and ticketing options, you can visit Metro Transit’s website.

Rideshare Services

Rideshare services such as Uber and Lyft are popular choices for concert-goers. These services provide convenient door-to-door transportation to U.S. Bank Stadium. The stadium has designated drop-off and pick-up locations for rideshare vehicles, ensuring a smooth and efficient experience. The designated spots are typically on 9th Avenue South between 6th Street South and 7th Street South, or on 3rd Street between Park Avenue and Portland Avenue. In case these areas are crowded, consider walking a bit farther into downtown to find an intersection away from the stadium. Avoiding 6th street is advisable, as it tends to fill up with cars quickly.

Parking Options

If you prefer to drive to the Taylor Swift concert, there are several parking options near U.S. Bank Stadium. While the stadium itself does not have a dedicated parking lot, there are public and private parking ramps available. You can find these ramps within close proximity to the stadium. Additionally, there is metered street parking available in the vicinity. It’s important to plan ahead and arrive early, especially as parking spots often fill up quickly. You can also check the mplsparking.com website for a comprehensive list of parking options and even reserve a parking spot in advance if preferred.

Overall, whether you choose public transportation, rideshare services, or parking near U.S. Bank Stadium, there are convenient options available to ensure you have a smooth and enjoyable experience getting to the Taylor Swift concert.
