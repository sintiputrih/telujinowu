Is Jhene Aiko Pregnant? Big Sean And Jhene Aiko Are Anticipating Their First Child Together: Report

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, January 1, 0001

Jhené Aiko and Big Sean, an American singer and rapper, began dating in 2016 and have since been dubbed one of the most beautiful couples in the public spotlight. Following the spread of rumors on social media, some fans believe the couple may welcome their first child together in 2022.

Neither of the stars has confirmed the baby rumors, and some fans are refusing to trust the allegations until further proof is offered. We look at why fans believe the 34-year-old singer is expecting her 2 children.

Who Is Jhene Aiko?

Aiko was born in the city of Los Angeles, California. Her family originally lived in South Los Angeles, but after her home was destroyed by fire when she was seven, they relocated to the View Park–Windsor Hills, Baldwin Hills, and Ladera Heights area near Slauson Avenue, where she was homeschooled until high school.

“Slauson Hills” is how she refers to her community. Christina Yamamoto and Karamo Chilombo, a physician, have a daughter named Aiko. Her parents are no longer married. Mila J, an R&B singer, is her sister.

Her mother has Spanish, Dominican, and Japanese ancestors, while her father has Native American, African-American, and German-Jewish ancestors. Her maternal grandfather is Japanese, and her maternal grandmother is Dominican Creole with Spanish roots.

She attended vocal training in Culver City, California, but stopped after she became pregnant suddenly. She gave birth to a baby girl named Namiko Love Browner when she was 20 years old.

Is Jhene Aiko Pregnant?

A fan reported having seen the pair at Whole Foods on Friday (June 17), where Aiko was displaying a prominent baby bump. “I just saw Jhené Aiko and Big Sean at Whole Foods and she’s obviously pregnant,” the tweet stated. Sean, Iker.”

While neither Big Sean nor Aiko have acknowledged the pregnancy on social media, insiders close to the couple have said that a baby is on the way. This will be Big Sean’s first child and Aiko’s second. Aiko’s recent Instagram images have further contributed to the commotion, with many pointing out that she’s wearing excessively baggy apparel, possibly to hide her growing tummy.

The couple had a brief breakup in March 2019, but Aiko continued to express her love for him. She left a lengthy Instagram remark on Big Sean’s post regarding Nipsey Hussle’s horrific death shortly after he was shot dead.
