Tess Harper Net Worth, Income, Salary, Earnings, Biography

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, January 14, 2024
Tess Harper (born August 15, 1950) is an actress from the United States. She was nominated for a Golden Globe Award for Best Supporting Actress for her debut film performance, in which she co-starred with Robert Duvall in Tender Mercies in 1983. As a result of her performance in the 1986 film Crimes of the Heart, she was nominated for an Academy Award in the Best Supporting Actress category. Her other films include Flashpoint (1984), Ishtar (1987), Far North (1988), and No Country for Old Men (1998). She has also appeared in a number of television shows (2007).
