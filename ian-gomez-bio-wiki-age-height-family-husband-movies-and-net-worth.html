Ian Gomez Career

From 1995 through 2004, Gomez appeared on The Drew Carey Show as Larry Almada, a coworker of Drew Carey’s character (also named Drew Carey) at Winfred-Louder.

He also appeared on Whose Line Is It Anyway? alongside Carey in 1999. He portrayed Javier Clemente Quintata on the WB drama Felicity from 1998 to 2002. Gomez appeared in Alanis Morissette’s “Hands Clean” music video in 2002.

In the same year, he starred in two episodes of the HBO series Curb Your Enthusiasm, alongside Jeff Greene, Ted Danson, Michael York, and Hugh Mellon, as the “bald chef” hired by Larry to cook in the restaurant he owned. Gomez starred in ABC’s Jake in Progress (2005–2006), alongside John Stamos.

In 2006, Gomez appeared in the fourth episode of Lost’s third season, “Every Man for Himself,” as well as as a psychiatrist in Reba’s April 2006 episode “Here We Go Again.”

From 2009 until 2015, Gomez played Andy Torres in the ABC and then TBS comedy Cougar Town, alongside fellow The Drew Carey Show castmate Christa Miller. Gomez and his then-wife, Nia Vardalos, co-hosted The Great American Baking Show, a reboot of The Great Holiday Baking Show, in 2016.

In 2019, he starred in Clint Eastwood’s film Richard Jewell. Gomez will play Luis Flores in the NBC comedy pilot Night School, based on the 2018 film and written by Christopher Moynihan, in 2020.
