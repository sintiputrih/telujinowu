18. Foxy Brown

Net worth: $1.8 Million

With total assets of $1.8 million, Foxy Brown managed to add her name to the top 20 richest female rappers globally.

Among the top 20 richest female rappers globally, MC Lyte secured the eleventh position as of 2023.

Apart from her rapping career, she is also an actress, voice-over artist, entrepreneur, and charity founder.

While talking about her acting career, Lyte appeared in various series such as Tales, S.W.A.T, In the House, and many more.

In 1996, she started her voice-over career with several brands such as Starz Network, The National League, AT & T, Diva Starz, and so on.

As a DJ, Lyte created the music for Google, Nissan, The Image Awards, and many others.

Furthermore, she is the founder of Sunni Gyrl Inc, an American entertainment management service.

Also, Lyte sells a mixture of vintage and new accessories like belts, glasses, and many others on her Los Angeles-based boutique Shaitel.

Hence, MC Lyte has an estimated net worth of $8 million as of [current year] through all these splendid careers.
