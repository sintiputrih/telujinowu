What Taylor Swift Song From Lover You Are, Based On Your Zodiac Sign

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, September 20, 2024

Taylor Swift is a legend in the music industry, and every single album she releases is total fire. With a snake symbolizing Reputation, Swift made the transition to a butterfly for her latest album, Lover. With 18 tracks, this album has stunning, fun, honest, and lovable music to choose from.

RELATED: Taylor Swift's 10 Most Incredible Career Accomplishments

For all die-hard fans of this talented bombshell, one of these songs might just be the perfect fit for their personality based on their zodiac sign. Whether you're an Aries or a Virgo, there's definitely one for everyone. Here's what song from Lover you are, based on your zodiac sign.

12 Aries: I Forgot That You Existed

This fire sign is a courageous, confident, and determined sign. They're all about passion, and their enthusiasm is unmatched. Of course, they're also known for being a bit moody or temperamental.

An Aries is a huge fan of love, but they also know their worth. This song is about being happy with oneself and leaving haters in the past. The confident Aries would love this for a breakup song.

11 Taurus: Daylight

This earth sign is well-grounded and sensual. They love worldly pleasures, and they're super amazing friends. They're reliable and devoted, and their patience is impressive.

A Taurus is all about love and earthly beauty, and that's basically what this song is about. It's about nature and daylight, but also about finding true love.

10 Gemini: London Boy

A Gemini is known for having two sides: the sociable and extroverted, and the serious and thoughtful. They're witty and fun, but they're also gentle and affectionate.

RELATED: Which Taylor Swift Instagram Look Are You Based On Your Zodiac?

This song is a fun and free-spirited tale of love. It's about dancing in the streets and being head over heels. A Gemini's fun and sociable side will love this tune, and it's also filled with affection and love.

9 Cancer: False God

This sign is known for being really emotional and sensitive. They're so loyal and caring, but they also have big imaginations and a tenacious attitude.

This is probably the rawest and honest song about love on the tracklist. It's about believing in love, and it's so sensual and emotional that all Cancers would definitely fall in love with it.

8 Leo: ME!

The Leo is represented by the Lion, which is the king of the jungle. This sign is full of leaders who are passionate and funny, but they're also dominant and confident.

This song is the ultimate self anthem. With so many fun lines and puns, and the fact that it's a collaboration with Panic! at the Disco's Brendon Urie, the hilarious Leo would definitely love it. Plus, Leo definitely thinks there's no one else like them. This is the song they're all jamming to.

7 Virgo: The Archer

This earth sign is one of the most hardworking and analytical signs, but they're also super intuitive and caring. They can be misunderstood, but their minds are wide and their hearts are deep.

RELATED: Taylor Swift's 10 Boldest Instagram Posts

This beautiful song is about fighting for oneself, and recognizing that everyone has good and bad. It's about fighting your inner fight and accepting yourself, and the hardworking but critical Virgo will deeply feel this song.

6 Libra: You Need To Calm Down

The Libra is the most social and fair of the zodiac signs. They love diplomacy and cooperation. They care about justice and peace, and they love being around others.

This is the most political of the songs on Swift's album, and it's all about justice and equality. It's an anthem for LGBTQ+ and women's rights, and the Libra will definitely be screaming this song at the top of their lungs. Plus, it's also all about friendship, another thing Libras will love about it.

5 Scorpio: I Think He Knows

This sign is about assertiveness and passion. They're brave and resourceful, but they're also totally stubborn. They can be pretty great leaders.

Out of the songs of Swift's new album, this one is about being totally confident and courageous when it comes to love. The passionate Scorpio loves risk, and this song is all about it.

4 Sagittarius: Soon You'll Get Better

This fire sign is the kindest for its type. They're generous, hilarious, and very idealistic. They're energetic and curious people, with optimism and open minds.

RELATED: 15 Lesser Known Facts About Taylor Swift’s Love Life

This song might be a bit slow for the energetic side of the Sagittarius, but it's also all about being optimistic and finding a silver lining. This gentle and motivating sign will certainly applaud this song.

3 Capricorn: The Man

The Capricorn is the hardest working of them all, and they can be a bit too serious at times. However, they're totally ambitious and are definitely set for success.

This sign has no time for roadblocks and setbacks, and they're definitely screaming this equality anthem. Like Swift's song, this sign is all about ambition and nothing will stop them from getting to the top.

2 Aquarius: Miss Americana & The Heartbreak Prince

This air sign can be quiet, but they can also be totally eccentric around the right people. They're independent people but are totally caring and progressive. They are also super intellectual.

This song is an upbeat tune about being authentically oneself and finding love amidst hate and whispers. The progressive and independent Aquarius doesn't let this get to them, they'll be having fun with their lover.

1 Pisces: Lover

Pisces are the most friendly of the signs. They're known for being artistic, compassionate, gentle, and selfless. Of course, they also rely deeply on being around others.

It's only fitting for this pure, sensual, and gentle sign to be the most like this stunning song. The Pisces is all about love, and this is the sweetest song on the album about romance. Every Pisces would listen to this on repeat.
