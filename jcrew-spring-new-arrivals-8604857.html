J.Crew Dropped 500+ Spring-Ready Styles, and I'm Buying These 5

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, January 1, 0001

Spring is one of my favorite seasons, specifically because of the fashion. It’s at the intersection of casual and elegant, allowing us to finally wear seasonal separates such as linen shirts, flats, and slip dresses. I’m currently revamping my wardrobe to match the season, so when I saw that J.Crew launched a new spring storefront with over 570 pieces, I was all in. Even as a picky fashion editor, I found five styles I’m adding to my cart ASAP — and they’re all under $150.  

5 New J.Crew Spring Arrivals 

Gamine Stretch Twill Shirtdress

How could I ignore the Gamine shirtdress? Spoiler alert: I couldn't. Made of primarily cotton, this utility dress boasts structured pockets, cuffed sleeves, a straight silhouette, and tortoise-shell buttons. I can’t get enough of the olive green hue, though it’s also available in pink and beige. 

Cashmere Shrunken T-Shirt

I’ve always been a sucker for cashmere; its buttery fabric and classy appearance is too good to pass up. So, it should come as no surprise that I’d add J.Crew’s Shrunken Cashmere T-shirt to my cart. It’s an elevated taken on a baby tee, with slightly elongated sleeves, a scoop neck, transitional fabric, and ribbed hemlines. Shop it in seven colors, such as cream, pink, blue, and black. 

Como Woven Straw Tote

I own too many totes for my own good… yet I still want another. The Como Woven Straw Tote is the do-it-all bag of my dreams, thanks to its spacious interior, 11-inch leather straps, and polished silhouette, which includes a flared top and shorter base. Throw your book, water bottle, and even laptop into the tote, and take it to the beach, farmers market, a date, picnic, and beyond. Choose from four strap variations. 

Slim Wide-Leg Jean

There’s no such thing as too many pants, so consider adding these refined bottoms to your rotation. Wearers can look forward to a slim fit throughout the thighs and a slightly wide leg opening, which echoes the likes of Katie Holmes and Dakota Johnson. Further details include gold hardware, a zippered closure, and functional pockets. Available in a soft beige color, the pants seamlessly pair with an array of hues and top styles.  

Capri Thong Leather Sandal

While spring officially starts on March 19, it’s not too early to snag a pair of these Capri sandals. Mimicking popular styles worn by Jennifer Lopez and Elle Fanning, the footwear features a square toe, leather straps, sleek footbed, and slight heel. Throw them on before running to your local coffee shop, or dress them up with a floral maxi dress. 

Shop J.Crew’s entire lineup of new spring arrivals here, and get ready to be the best dressed.
