Sophie Gregoire Trudeau, wife of Canadian Prime Minister Justin Trudeau.

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, March 28, 2024

If you are used to watching Canadian politics news, then Sophie Gregoire Trudeau must not be a new name to you. Sophie Gregoire Trudeau is the wife of famous Canadian Prime-Minister, Justin Trudeau. Sophie has been married for more than fifteen years. Recently, Sophie tested positive for the Coronavirus.

Sophie Gregoire Trudeau was born as Gregoire Trudeau on April 24, 1975, in Montreal, Quebec. She is the only child born to Jean Gregoire and his wife, Estelle Blais. Her father was a stockbroker, and her mother worked as a Nurse. Sophie met Justin when in school.

Relationship Status of Sophie Gregoire

Sophie Gregorie is a married woman. She is married to her childhood friend, Justin Trudeau. The couple first met in their school and have been together ever since. Sophie tied his knot with his long time boyfriend, Justin Trudeau, on May 28, 2005, in a Catholic wedding at the Sainte-Madeleine Outremont Church in Montreal. 

The couple is the proud parents of three children. They welcomed their first son, Xavier James Trudeau, on October 18, 2007. Soon after that, Ella was born on February 5, 2009. Then Hadrien Trudeau was born on February 28, 2014. 

Memorable and Lavish Wedding

As per the reports of CBC, Trudeau wore a light-colored suit, while Gregoire wore an ecru dress with lace motifs and a long veil trimmed in the same lace that billowed behind her as she stepped slowly down the stairs.

After the wedding, the love birds drove away in Pierre Trudeau's 1960 Mercedes Benz 300 SL roadster, which was shipped from a B.C. auto restorer to Montreal. The groom's brother, Alexandre (Sacha) Trudeau, served as best man while his mother, Margaret, looked on.

The wedding was held privately. Only close family members and a number of family friends and members from congress were invited to the ceremony. 

How rich is Sophie Gregoire?

Sophie Gregoire has an estimated net worth of $1 Million. She worked as a T.V Show host and gossip reporter for almost a decade. More to that, in 2012, she became a certified yoga Instructor. As of now, she is busy with her charity works. 

Sophie Grégoire-Trudeau is Canada's latest fashion icon

Also, her husband, Justin Trudeau, is among the top 100 powerful people in the world with a net worth of more than $13 Million. He was sworn in as the Prime Minister of Canada on November 4, 2015. The family of five have been there in the Prime Minister Office ever since, Being the Prime-Minister, Justin receives, $345,000 as his annual salary and also gets a $2,000 car allowance, and a variety of staff that is paid for with taxpayer dollars. 

Sophie along with her husband Tested Positive for Covid-19

Yes, You read that correct, Sophie, along with Justin, have been tested positive for the virus Covid-19. As Gregoire had been to Britain with he daughter Ella-Grace, she is suspected of having caught the virus there.

They will remain in isolation for the next two weeks. Ms. Grégoire Trudeau became mildly ill last Wednesday, and physicians tested her for the virus after she developed a low fever, which has since passed. Gregoire and Trudeau voluntarily isolated themselves on Thursday.
