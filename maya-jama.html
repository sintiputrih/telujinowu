Maya Jama Height, Weight, Age, Husband, Biography, Family, More

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, June 25, 2024

Maya Jama (born August 14, 1994) is an English Model, Television Host, Radio Presenter, Instagram Star, and Social Media Personality from Bristol. She is famous for being the current presenter of MTV and Channel 4.

Earlier, Maya used to work as a co-presenter of “Trending Live!” on 4Music. Furthermore, the host worked there from 2015 until 2017. Later, Jama started working on a game show Cannonball on ITV in 2017.

Currently, she presents a BBC Radio 1 show on Fridays and Saturdays 10 am – 1 pm. Moreover, the TV host also works in the MTV show True Love or True Lies along with Danny Dyer.

The young television personality also works with Alan Carr and Adam Hills in the project called Stand Up to Cancer. She also collaborated with fashion brand Pretty Little Thing in 2018 and launched her own collection.

Personal Affairs, Boyfriend & Love Life

The 25-years-old Maya Jama had a romantic relationship with her ex-boyfriend, Stormzy. He is a British rapper, singer, and songwriter whose real name is Michael Ebenazer Kwadjo Omari Owuo Jr.

They remained together for more than 4 years. However, they never revealed anything about their love life, how they first met, and personal life on the internet. Michael called off their love affair and got separated in 2019.
