Christopher Noel Dorsey, better known by his stage name B.G., is an American rapper hailing from New Orleans, Louisiana. Read this article to find out more about his net worth and discography.

B.G. the rapper, was born on September 3, 1980, in the United States of America. He emerged as a prominent figure in the hip-hop scene during the 1990s and early 2000s.

B.G. started his rap journey as a member of the hip-hop group “Cash Money Millionaires,” which was part of the Cash Money Records label.

He made his debut in 1995 with the album “True Story,” which showcased his lyrical prowess and established him as a rising star in the rap industry.

B.G.’s passion for rap music ignited during his high school years, and he wasted no time in embarking on his musical journey. He swiftly became a recognizable figure after aligning himself with Cash Money Records.

Intriguingly, Christopher’s affiliation with Cash Money Records commenced when he was just 12 years old, marking the beginning of his remarkable musical career.

One of B.G.’s most significant career milestones came with the release of his fifth studio album, “Chopper City in the Ghetto,” in 1999.

The album featured the hit single “Bling Bling,” which not only became a chart-topping success but also popularized the phrase “bling bling” in hip-hop culture.

Throughout his career, B.G. continued to release successful albums, including “Livin’ Legend” (2003), “The Heart of tha Streetz, Vol. 2” (2006), and “Too Hood 2 Be Hollywood” (2009).

However, B.G.’s career faced some challenges. In 2012, he was sentenced to 14 years in federal prison on weapons possession and witness tampering charges.

B.G. Arrest and Imprisonment
