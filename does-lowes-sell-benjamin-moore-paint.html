This brand has some lovely unique colors and is of very high quality. There are also some blends that have actually been designed to be used on a number of surfaces, not just one specific type. So instead of needing two or three separate tins, you can get your multi-surface job done with just one!

A Little More On Lowe’s 

Lowes is a big name in home improvement, DIY projects, and all types of paint. You’ll be able to find a store in 24 different countries. Lowes not only has a large presence in the United States with 2,197 stores across all 50 states, but also has stores in the United Kingdom, Canada, Austria, Germany, Mexico, Japan, Italy, Holland, and many more.

They boast a huge range of supplies accessible in-store for a fun and easy shopping trip, plus a very large catalog available on the Lowes website. You can peruse a wide variety of hardware, building materials, lighting, garden tools and plants, air conditioners, and a variety of paint.

Does Lowes Sell Benjamin Moore Paint?

Unfortunately, no, Lowes does not stock Benjamin Moore’s Paint range. There were rumors that Lowes had won a contract with this specialty paint company. However, Warren Buffett, owner of Berkshire Hathaway and therefore Benjamin Moore, decided to rule against his CEO and this agreement. Meaning he was able to keep the promise he made back in 2000 during the acquisition of the company. 

In turn, this kept the high-end specialty Benjamin Moore products out of big box stores like Lowes and its direct competitor, Home Depot. This paint is only available from smaller, independently owned authorized retailers and hardware stores, of which there are over 7,500 locations across North America.

What Paint Does Lowes Sell?

Lowes has a range of alternative paint brands in stock to Benjamin Moore, which you can check out in-store or browse online for added convenience.

These include:

    • HGTV Home by Sherwin Williams – Sherwin Williams has an exclusive contract with Lowe’s, so HGTV Home Showcase and HGTV Home Ovation Interior paints can be purchased only at Lowe’s and Sherwin Williams storefronts.
    • Valspar – A well known and industry-leading paint that is part of the Sherwin Williams brand. 
    • Magnolia Homes by Joanna Gaines – Signature colors and finishes from one of Home Improvement TVs’ most loved interior designers, working in conjunction with KILZ paint brand.

Can Lowes Colour Match A Benjamin Moore’s Paint Colour?

Indeed they can! Did you know Lowes also provides a fantastic paint color matching service available in-store? 

So if you can’t find the exact color on the shelves or on a paint chip that you want, one of Lowes’s associates can create it for you. This service costs a little more than the usual price of a can of paint, and you’ll need to verify the exact cost and your local shop, and it varies at each individual store.

Is It Simple To Colour Match Paint At Lowes?

Yes, it’s a pretty simple process. All you need to do is bring along a color sample, paint chip, or even a piece of furniture with the color you require and head to the paint department at Lowes.

Hand it over to the staff member there, and they will utilize their color matching tool, the X-Rite iVue Machine, to help you. This machine will scan and read the color you provide and then create a tint to replicate it perfectly. You will be able to decide with the employee the size of paint can and finish you need.

Is Benjamin Moore A Good Brand Of Paint?

Yes, it is really good quality, high-end paint. And there are a few different versions available, depending on what and where you are painting.  

These include Aura Interior, Aura Bath and Spa, Regal Select Interior, Ben Interior, and Advance Interior. 

All have the benefits of:

  • Long-lasting Proprietary Color-Lock Technology.
  • Scuff and burnish resistance.
  • Mildew resistant.
  • Primer and color combined.
  • Uniform color and fade resistance and extreme color depth and richness.
  • Easy cleaning.
  • Quick, seamless drying and touchups.

For example, if you are painting your bathroom or live in a high humidity environment, then the company’s Aura Bath and Spa luxurious matte paint is the perfect paint and finish for you. You can usually pick up a quart tin of paint for around $40 and a gallon for under $90.

Does Lowes Sell Benjamin Moore Paint – Final Thoughts

Unfortunately, you cannot buy Benjamin Moore paint or stains at big, multi-chain superstores like Lowe’s or Home Depot. This is because they want to keep the integrity of their boutique-style business by only being stocked in selected independent retailers and hardware stores.

However, Lowe’s offers several other paint alternatives with a multitude of colors and finishes. And, if you really want a particular Benjamin Moore color and aren’t near one of their stockists. Don’t forget the awesome service of in-store paint color matching Lowe’s offers its customers.
