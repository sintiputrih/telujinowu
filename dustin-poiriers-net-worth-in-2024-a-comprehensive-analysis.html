Overview of Dustin Poirier

Dustin Glenn Poirier, born on January 19, 1989, in Lafayette, Louisiana, is an American professional mixed martial artist who competes in the Lightweight division of the UFC. Known by his moniker "The Diamond," Poirier's journey from a young fighter in Louisiana to a top contender in the UFC's Lightweight division is a testament to his resilience, determination, and skill.
