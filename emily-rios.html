Biography Timeline

Emily Rios is a Mexican-American actress best known for her roles in the television series “The Bridge” and “Snowfall”. Here is a detailed biography timeline of Emily Rios:

  • April 27, 1989: Emily Rios was born to Mexican immigrant parents in Los Angeles, California.
  • 2006: Rios is discovered by a talent scout at her high school and begins her acting career with a role in the independent film “Quinceañera”.
  • 2007: Rios receives critical acclaim for her performance in “Quinceañera” and wins the Sundance Film Festival’s Jury Prize for Acting.
  • 2008: Rios appears in the television series “Friday Night Lights” and the film “Vicious Circle”.
  • 2010: Rios appears in the films “Down for Life” and “Breaking Point”.
  • 2011: Rios appears in the films “The Trouble with Bliss” and “Enter the Dangerous Mind”.
  • 2013: Rios joins the cast of the television series “The Bridge” and appears in the film “The Return of Joe Rich”.
  • 2015: Rios appears in the films “Collisions” and “The Night Is Young”.
  • 2017: Rios appears in the television series “From Dusk till Dawn: The Series” and the film “Burning Sands”.
  • 2019: Rios joins the cast of the television series “Snowfall” as Lucia Villanueva.
