Who is Ethan Mbappé?

Ethan Mbappé Lottin is a French footballer who plays for Paris Saint-German as a midfielder. He has three years contract with PSG.

Mbappe has been a center of attraction nearly on every social media platform. He comes from a sports family background, so he started playing football at a very young age.

Ethan Mbappé: Age, Parents, Siblings

Ethan Mbappé was born on 29th December 2006 in Paris. His father’s name is Wilfried Mbappé and his mother is Fayza Mbappé.

His dad is Cameroonian and his mother is of Algerian descent. He also has two brothers named Jires Kembo Ekoko and Kylian Mbappé.

His father is a football coach where as his mother was a former handball player. His older brother, Kylian Mbappé, is a famous football player who was adopted by his parents.

Apart from this, Jires Kembo Ekoko whom the Mbappé family adopted to the family when he was a kid and came to Europe for education was a former footballer.

Education, School/College University

Ethan Mbappé is a young player who has played for the PSG junior squad for several years. According to some sources, Mbappe is still in high school. However, the ware about his school/college name is still unknown.

Ethan Mbappé: Professional Life, Career

Ethan comes from a sports family background and he started playing football at a very young age. In 2015, at the age of 12, he played his first professional match in Monaco for the Ligue 1 club as a senior. He has signed a contract with PSG.

Mbappe is working hard to be on the topper’s list as he is now a part of the Paris Saint-German academy for a couple of years. Mbappe has signed a contract with the capital club for the next seasons, till 2024 he would be playing for Paris Saints.
