Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, September 9, 2024

Bury the Bride Tubi cast includes Krsy Fox, Dylan Rourke, and Scout Taylor-Compton. The movie is set to be released on Tubi on April 22, 2023.

The streaming service has a special treat for its users coming Saturday with the release of the newest horror thriller, Bury the Bride, directed and co-written by Spider One alongside Fox.

Besides, Tubi offers the largest library of content with 50,000 plus movies and television shows to watch. It is one of the leading free, premium, on-demand video streaming platforms of all time.

The new Tubi horror movie focuses on a bride-to-be and her friends whose bachelorette weekend takes a deadly turn when the fiance and his friends come to crash the fun party.

Ahead of the release of the film this week, the streaming service shared its poster and trailer, and fans can’t stop gushing about it.

Many individuals have even made movie review videos on YouTube for the upcoming release. The film will surely give its viewers a chilling experience and goosebumps with its thrilling content.

Meet The Cast Of Bury The Bride

Bury the Bride Tubi cast include Scout Taylor-Compton, Krsy Fox, Dylan Rourke, and Chaz Bono in leading roles. Lyndsi LaRose, Rachel Brunner, and Katie Ryan also star in this horror thriller.

The movie features a small and contained cast, each depicting significant roles.

Krsy Fox as Sadie Hamilton

Fox is a 39-year-old actress and filmmaker from Campbell River, Canada. She began her career at 12 and has gone on to direct, write, and star in some of the hit titles.

She is best known for her appearance in the award-winning short film What The Spell? as Ryan in 2020. She is also notable for portraying Ruby Chase on Frank and Carolyn on I Live Alone in 2021.

The actress stars in the 2022 horror film Allegoria released through Utopia, Shudder, and RLJE, which is receiving rave reviews.

Besides acting, she has had a lucrative music career. She wrote several No. 1 radio hits with other brands and fronted her band Knee High Fox, with whom she toured North America extensively.

Scout Taylor-Compton as June Hamilton

Taylor-Compton portrays bride-to-be June, who goes on a bachelorette weekend with her girlfriends in Bury the Bride.

Scout is an American actress who has frequently worked in the horror genre, most notably in the 2007 remake of Halloween and its 2009 sequel Halloween II as Laurie Strode.

Outside of horror, the 34-year-old actress played the role of Lita Ford in the 2010 biographical drama film The Runaways.

Born to a Mexican mother and a mortician father, Compton was raised in Long Beach, California. She said she grew up in the whole horror genre and visited her father at the mortuary.

As of writing, Taylor-Compton has added 103 credits to her name as an actress.

Dylan Rourke as David

Rourke is an American stage, film, and television actor. He was an accomplished scholar who graduated from Hamilton High School and attended Arizona State University (ASU).

At ASU, he studied Business Communication but his passion took in the performing arts.

He took training in theatre stage performing and improvisational comedy. His performing arts training began in California.

Rourke made his screen debut in 2013 in the short drama Lone Wolf. Further appearances came steadily after his debut in films and series such as Mistresses, Modern Family, and Grey’s Anatomy.

Chaz Bono as Puppy

Bono, the son of entertainers Sonny Bono and Cher, is an American writer, actor, and musician. He gained recognition following his appearance as a child in The Sonny & Cher Comedy Hour.

He began a short music career with the Banc Ceremony in 1988. The band released one album, Hang Out Your Poetry, in 1993. The band featured Chaz on vocals, percussion, and acoustic guitar.

In the acting world, he is best recognized for his work on Curb Your Enthusiasm and American Horror Story. He has written three books, including New York Time Best Seller Family Outing 1998.

Bury The Bride Movie Review

Bury the Bride had its world premiere at Panic Fest in Kansas City on April 15, 2023, followed by Slaem Horror Film Fest on April 22.

The movie centers on June, played by Compton, who is getting married. She alongside her sister and friends goes to a remote place suggested by her fiance to have a fun bachelorette party.

In the beginning, the film gives viewers a vibe of a girl-type movie -you have the nerdy friend and the hot one too. June is engaged to a man whom she and her friends have never met.

The girls start partying and all of a sudden her fiance and some of his friends show up unexpectedly. Now the joyful party takes a deadly turn and what follows becomes a living nightmare.

June, her sister, Sadie, and their friends must fight for their lives.

The movie has an unexpected turn through halfway which will surely blow up your mind.

The chemistry between the characters is remarkable as many stars have previously worked together.

Another notable part of the movie is the place where the girls go for a party. It will make us wonder why these beautiful girls are going to such a remote and weird place to have their party.

Well, all the questions are answered in the later part of the movie. There are so many surprises, twists, and plots in this horror thriller which adds it to the must-watch list.
