What is Economy Plus on a train?

Economy Class + cars are designed to carry 51 passengers and offer more space as well as one power socket for charging mobile appliances per two seats. ​Usually there is only one Economy Class Plus coach on each train train and it is the #10 coach, if the trainset is doubled, it can be the #20 coach as well.

What is the difference between escape and economy on VIA Rail?

The difference between the two rates can be found by hovering your computer mouse over the fare column header. There are not only luggage restrictions, but also cancellation & ticket exchange rules. Escape fares are non-refundable, economy fares are refundable.

What is a sleeper plus on VIA Rail?

By Night. Sleeper Plus offers the added comfort of amenities, great service and your choice of a cosy berth, a cabin for one, two or four.

Can you shower on VIA Rail?

Shower amenities are provided in your cabin (towels, shampoo and soap). Additional amenities are available in Prestige class. At VIA Rail, safety is our top priority. Our onboard staff will take you through our procedures shortly after boarding.

Can you bring alcohol on VIA Rail?

Can we bring our personal alcoholic drinks on board? No, Provincial laws forbid passengers from consuming personal alcoholic drinks on the trains. Personal alcoholic drinks may only be consumed inside private sleeping car cabins on some trains.

What is a cabin for 2 on VIA Rail?

A cabin for two is designed for two people. Other amenities: small closet, drinking water, paper and towels, pillows, sheets and blankets supplied.

Can you smoke on the Canadian train?

Tobacco. Smoking is prohibited on board all VIA Rail trains and in public places in all major Canadian cities. Those who wish to smoke while travelling with VIA may be able to do so at certain station stops.

Where are the outlets on VIA Rail trains?

There are at-your-seat power outlets in Economy class on the Renaissance train cars. However, there are no power outlets in the service car. There are also power outlets in your private cabin aboard other VIA Rail trains.
