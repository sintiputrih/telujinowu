Is Bijan Robinson Hair Real? Long Hairstyle Of The NFL Draft

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, June 22, 2024

Bijan Robinson hair has been a topic of interest for his fans and supporters. They are curious to know whether his hair is natural or not. Please keep on reading to find that out.

Ranked a five-star recruit, the Arizona-born player played his college football for the Texas Leghorns.

In 2022, he received the Doak Walker Award as the nation’s top running back and also was named a Unanimous All-American in the same year.

In addition, he earned the 2020 Alamo Bowl MVP with 10 rushes for 183 yards and a touchdown. 

Before playing for the Texas Longhorns, he attended Salpointe Catholic High School for his high school.

During his high school career, he set a state record after rushing for 7,036 yards and completing 114 touchdowns.

Due to his exceptional performance, the running back player was selected to play in the 2020 All-American Bowl.

Is Bijan Robinson Hair Real?

Bijan Robinson has showcased his uniqueness when it comes to hair styling. Along with his skills on the pitch, his unique hairstyle also often gets the limelight.

The hairstyle of the NFL draft star has added more composition to his physical appearance.

He is known for his distinctive hairstyle that includes a long braid. The player makes bulky hair on the upper part of the head and ties it at the back.

His transition from short hairstyles to dreads has left many surprised. Many people are even suspicious of whether the hair is real or fake.

However, based on various images and videos of him available online, his hair appears natural and not a wig or hair extension.

Before 2020, he frequently used to have short dreads, as seen in his Instagram account.

However, his signature look now is a long braid, which he flaunts on and off the playground.

When it comes to color, Robinson has maintained his original color, black. Unlike most players, he does not seem to be interested in changing his hair color.

The football player is attentive toward his hair which also reflects his sense of caring for his body and discipline as well.

Bijan Robinson: Parents

Bijan Robinson was born on January 30, 2002, in Tucson, Arizona, United States.

He was born when his mother, Lamore, was just a college student. The details of his biological father are currently unknown.

LaMore is licensed to perform aesthetics services, and in addition to that, she works as a makeup artist and distributor for It Works.

His mother, Lamore, married Lamont Sauls in 2012 when the player was ten years old.

Bijan chose to stay with his grandparents instead with his mother and stepfather.

Cleo Robinson, the player’s grandfather, has played a significant role in shaping his professional life.

Their relationship is beyond the typical grandfather-grandson dynamic, closer to father-son. Bijan even calls him Dad.

When asked about his grandson, Cleo said, “He’s the person I’ve been admiring all the time”.

Not only his grandfather but Robinson’s grandmother also loves to watch her husband and grandson playing football. 

The contribution made by his grandparents has helped the player what he has been able to achieve to date.
