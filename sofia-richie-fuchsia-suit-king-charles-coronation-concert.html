Sofia Richie Wears a Fuchsia Suit To King Charles's Coronation Concert

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, March 10, 2024

Sofia Richie has chosen a chic, eye-catching look for tonight's Coronation Concert.

The model and newlywed is attending in support of her father, Lionel Richie, who will perform at the special musical celebration along with stars including Katy Perry and Andrea Bocelli. For the event, the newlywed gave a modern spin on the prim military-style suit comprised of a metallic fuchsia cropped jacket and matching high-waisted trousers by Destree.

The glam co-ord features vintage-inspired detailing, including a high neck, defined seams, and structured puffed shoulders. She completed the look with black pointed-toe Saint Laurent heels, a black top-handle bag with a decorative gold clasp, and gold pendant hoop earrings.

Last month, Richie tied the knot with music executive Elliot Grainge in a lavish wedding in the South of France. The nuptials brought in a star-studded crowd, which included the model's sister, Nicole Richie, dad Lionel Richie, and longtime friends Paris Hilton and Cameron Diaz. The multi-day affair provided a showcase of elegant ensembles, including several wedding weekend looks and the three bespoke bridal dresses that Chanel made her for the rehearsal dinner, ceremony, and reception.

The "All Night Long" singer recently opened up about walking his daughter down the aisle, revealing that he's known Grainge since the groom was nine years old.

"It was a moment where, yes, I was letting her go, but I was also gaining a family," he told Extra of the emotional day. "She spent the whole time walking down the aisle: 'I love you dad, I love you dad, I love you dad,' and I go... 'You're killing me. You're killing me.' But that's what a dad wants to hear, and I love Baby Girl so much, and the family was there to root her on."
