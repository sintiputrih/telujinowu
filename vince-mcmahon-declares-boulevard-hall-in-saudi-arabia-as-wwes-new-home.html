Vince McMahon Declares Boulevard Hall in Saudi Arabia as WWEs New Home

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, March 5, 2024

Vince McMahon and The Undertaker were spotted in Saudi Arabia to attend the highly-anticipated boxing showdown between Tyson Fury and Francis Ngannou. The fight took place at Boulevard Hall and was accompanied by various spectacular performances and musical acts, drawing star-studded audience that included Eminem, Conor McGregor, and Kanye West.

In an interview with MMAJunkie, Vince McMahon expressed his excitement about the fight and praised Saudi Arabia’s dedication to both combat sports and entertainment. He emphasized that Saudi Arabia has become a significant hub for world-class sports entertainment, indicating WWE’s commitment to the region.

“Everything they’re doing is so much. They’re delving into the superior aspects of all of sports entertainment. There is nothing going to bring.. Everything that is the best in the world, it’s going to be here.”

“This is our home now. This, as been pronounced, this our home, WWE’s home.”

The Undertaker also shared his positive sentiments about Saudi Arabia, commending the country’s efforts to bring top-tier sporting events and entertainment to the region. He acknowledged the dedication Saudi Arabia has shown in delivering outstanding experiences for fans.

WWE has been holding major events in Saudi Arabia as part of their long-term partnership with the country. Their next event in Saudi Arabia, WWE Crown Jewel, is scheduled for November 4, continuing their strong presence in the region.
