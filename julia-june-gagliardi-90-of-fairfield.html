Julia June Gagliardi, 90, Of Fairfield

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, September 15, 2024

Gagliardi was born in Rossiter, Pa., to Joseph and Clara Colli Realini. She retired in 1987 after 35 years of service from the Stamford Tool and Die Customatic.

Gagliardi is survived by her daughter, Roberta Brandt (Ronald) of Fairfield; three grandchildren; seven great-grandchildren; Barbara Enright of Stamford who was like a second daughter; brother-in-law, Walter Clark (Duke) of Milford; sister-in-law, Anne Gagliardi of Spring Hill, Fla.; and several nieces and nephews. She was predeceased by her parents; husband, James Gagliardi; sister, Clementina (Tina) Clark; and brothers-in-law, Frank (Buster) Gagliardi and Fred Gagliardi.

Funeral services will be held privately.

Memorial donations may be directed to AIND/Giant Steps, 309 Barberry Road, Southport, CT 06890 or The Jewish Home for the Elderly, 175 Jefferson St., Fairfield, CT 06825.

For more information, visit http://www.leskopolkefuneralhome.com
