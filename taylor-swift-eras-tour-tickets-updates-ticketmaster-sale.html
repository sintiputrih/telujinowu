How to avoid scammers

If there are any spelling or grammatical errors on your ticket, then it is definitely a scam.

Also, it's a good idea to check a map of the venue before buying a ticket to see if the seat numbers match up.

Finally, don't send money using apps like Zelle or Venmo, which have fewer protections for scam victims. It's better to use PayPal instead.
