Ted Sarandos Net Worth – $240 million

What is Ted Sarandos’ net worth? Who is Ted Sarandos’ spouse?

Introduction

Ted Sarandos is the co-chief officer and chief content officer for Netflix.

He was promoted to this role in 2020, but has worked for Netflix since 2000.

From early on, Sarandos had a vision for Netflix – to make under distributed movies, such as foreign language movies, movies from the indie genre, and intimate stories, as accessible as possible on the platform.

Netflix is the world’s largest and most ambitious subscription streaming service that offers a wide variety of shows and movies.

Netflix was founded by Reed Hastings and Marc Randolph in 1997 and originally started as a service that distributed DVDs.

Under Sarandos’s leadership, Netflix received 54 Primetime Emmy Awards nominations in 2016.

Sarandos’s honors and accolades include: “Digital Power 50” for 2010 and 2012 in The Hollywood Reporter, “Time 100” in 2013, the Humanitarian Award (2014), Pioneer Award from the International Documentary Association, “Silicon Beach Power 25” in The Hollywood Reporter, the Brandon Tartikoff Legacy Award (2015), and Honorary Member of The Harvard Lampoon – to name a few. 

The Origin Story

@Getty

He was born on the 30th of July 1964 and raised in Phoenix, Arizona, and is of Greek heritage.

”My grandfather on my father’s side is Alex Cariotacas,” Ted said in an interview discussing the ambitious production line for Netflix and its rapid expansion with Greek Reporter. Sarandos added: ”He came to America as a teenager and changed his name to Sarandos, from Samos, Greece.”

He grew up in a big working-class family; his father was an electrician, and his mother was a stay-at-home mum, taking care of five children.

Ted has three older sisters and one younger brother.

In his words, it was a “chaotic household with five kids running around.”

Sarandos, who felt no routine, found peace in watching TV and organizing his day around when his favorite shows were on.

He spent copious hours of his childhood in front of the TV; this schedule ruled his life.

Late at night, until the early hours of the morning, Sarandos was glued to comedy shows such as The Jack Benny Show and I Love Lucy and would get no more than five hours of sleep.

It clearly didn’t do the man any harm – he is now a multi-millionaire in the heart of the film industry and is responsible for revolutionizing the way we watch movies.

He has been described as “the future of Netflix” for his keen eye in pulling in blockbusters to the streaming platform.

His love of movies continued to grow throughout his childhood.

He often tells a story in interviews where he recalls his first experience of the theatre:

”I love the theatre. I remember vividly seeing that Blazing Saddles was the first r-rated movie I ever saw in a movie theatre – I was ten years old – and I thought I’d robbed a bank, I got away with this thing – I remember that experience so vividly.”

Education and Early Career

@Getty

Ted attended Alhambra High School, Arizona State University, and Glendale Community College.

In the 1980s, during Ted’s early 20s, Ted worked part-time at a strip mall video store whilst studying.

By aged 30, Ted became an executive for a company that supplied Blockbuster videos.

Netflix

It was in 2000 that Sarandos was hired by Netflix, back when the international streaming platform was posting rental DVDs to customers.

It was Sarandos’s role to develop this service.

In January 2023, Sarandos said that Netflix has “never canceled a successful show.”

Wife

Sarandos was married to Michelle Sarandos and has two children with her – Sarah and Tony Sarandos.

By 2008 Ted was divorced, his children teenagers at the time.

In 2009, Ted Sarandos married Nicole Avant, a former United States Ambassador to the Bahamas.

Real Estate

In April 2021, Ted sold his 14.7 million Malibu Beach House.

He sold his second Malibu home in 2018 to Robbie Williams for $22,000,000 and bought a home in Montecito from Ellen Degeneres for $37.000,000.

Ted and his wife Nicole formerly lived in Beverly Hills in a home that was listed for $8,000,000.

In 2017, they moved to Hancock Peak, LA, in a mansion that was sold to them for $15,497,000, a home previously owned by Melanie Griffith and Antonio Banderas.

Sarandos’s success in the movie industry has been incredible.

As well as being the Co-CEO and Chief Content Officer of Netflix, leading content acquisition for the streaming platform since 2000, Sarandos is a member of Spotify’s board of directors and had been since 2016.

He has been described as “the human algorithm” because of his encyclopedic knowledge of movies and his startling ability to predict what people want to watch.

Half of Sarandos’s compensation was his $20,000,000 salary.

In 2021, Sarandos accrued 17.1 million in stock awards and $20 million in salary.
