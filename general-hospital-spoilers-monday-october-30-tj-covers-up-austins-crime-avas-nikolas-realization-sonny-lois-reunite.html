General Hospital Spoilers: Monday, October 30 TJ Covers Up Austins Crime Avas Nikolas

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, February 20, 2024

General Hospital (GH) spoilers for Monday, October 30, reveal that TJ Ashford (Tajh Bellow) will have a big decision to make. When TJ realized Mason Gatlin (Nathanyael Grey) wouldn’t be prosecuted for kidnapping him, he fumed over the lack of justice.

However, TJ later found himself outside Mason’s hospital room watching Austin Gatlin-Holt (Roger Howarth) smother his cousin with a pillow.

Although it goes against everything believes as a doctor, the personal side of TJ that wants payback may win out. TJ may decide to let this crime happen and cover for Austin once the deed is done.

GH spoilers say Austin will get drunk on Monday’s episode, so he obviously won’t be taken into police custody for taking out Mason.

If TJ were to intervene and keep Mason alive, then Mason would surely rat Austin out to the cops.

It seems like the only way Austin gets away with this now is if he finishes the job and trusts that TJ will keep his mouth shut in the aftermath.

Although Austin hates Mason’s guts, taking his cousin’s life could still take a toll on him.

Austin will hate that he keeps getting forced into terrible situations, so it’s easy to see why he’ll want to numb everything with alcohol.

Meanwhile, Laura Collins (Genie Francis) will catch up with Ava Jerome (Maura West), who’ll be surprised to learn there’s proof that Nikolas Cassadine (Adam Huss) is alive and that he’s been in Geneva.

The wheels may start turning for Ava, who could eventually realize Austin lied to her about what happened the night he helped her with Nikolas.

Monday’s GH preview video shows Ava sternly summoning someone to the gallery NOW, so she may call Austin over and demand the truth.

In Vermont, Elizabeth Baldwin (Rebecca Herbst) and Hamilton Finn (Michael Easton) will let more sparks fly as they grow even closer, so it sounds like they may finally hit the sheets.

Back in Port Charles, Brook Lynn Quartermaine (Amanda Setton) will apparently turn down a drink and tell Kristina Corinthos-Davis (Kate Mansi) that she needs a clear head for what she’s about to do.

While Brook Lynn plans to handle Tracy Quartermaine (Jane Elliot), Sam McCall (Kelly Monaco) will get Cody Bell (Josh Kelly) and Sasha Gilmore (Sofia Mattsson) to help her with a project – perhaps something special for Halloween.

As for Lucy Coe (Lynn Herring), she’ll make a bold declaration to Martin Grey (Michael E. Knight).

Lucy will decide she needs to show Tracy exactly who she’s dealing with, so she’ll take a shocking stand.

Next, “Eddie Maine” aka Ned Quartermaine (Wally Kurth) will talk about refusing to take no for an answer.

There’ll be some bickering and frustration on both sides during “Eddie’s” conversation with Tracy.

Olivia Quartermaine (Lisa LoCicero) will end up rushing to “Eddie’s” defense, so he should appreciate having her in his corner.

Sonny Corinthos (Maurice Benard) will also have a heartfelt reunion with Lois Cerullo (Rena Sofer), who’ll get updates on all the major developments in his life – including his new marriage to Nina Corinthos (Cynthia Watros).

Lois will ask how something’s working out for Sonny, so she’ll push for honesty and offer the same when she presumably offers advice.

General Hospital spoilers say Sonny will enjoy reconnecting with Lois, but he’ll have to focus on Cyrus Renault (Jeff Kober) and more troubling news later.
