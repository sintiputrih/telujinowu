Is Brian Landry Found Dead? Update On Missing Gabby Petito Boyfriend

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, May 1, 2024

The mysterious disappearance of 22-year-old Gabby Petito has gripped the whole nation. Now, her fiance, Brian, is also missing. Is Brian Landry found dead?

Gabby Petito went missing while she was on a cross-country trip with her fiance, Brian Landry. The pair were road-tripping in a van over the summer.

In fact, they regularly posted photos on their social media with the hashtag #vanlife. However, the posts stopped in August.

Then, Brian returned home on September 1 alone in his van. Petito was not with him even though they left together.

Petito’s parents file a missing complaint on September 11 and police discovered a dead body in Wyoming resembling Petito’s structure a few days ago.

Now, the autopsy confirmed the body is of Ms. Gabby Petito, and Brian is the person of interest in her alleged murder, reported Fox 8.

Is Brian Landry Found Dead?

Brian Landry is not found dead. However, his missing fiance has been confirmed dead by the authorities.

The autopsy result confirmed that the remains found in Wyoming are of Gabby Petito. The exact cause of her death is not yet disclosed but the police have ruled the death as a homicide.

The 23-year-old Landry and Petitio were childhood sweethearts and met while growing up in Long Island before he moved to North Port with his family.

His parents, Chris and Roberta, own a 10,000 square feet property in the North Port area.

The police went to his home on September 17, the day when he went missing himself, to investigate the case. They removed a car and other items from the property.

Did They Find Brian Landry? Where Is He Now?

No, Brian Landry is still missing. His whereabouts are unknown as of now.

He went missing on September 17, a week after Petito’s family reported her missing. The police identified him as a person of interest in Petito’s murder and are in search of him.

The police also discovered a note on the windshield of a car they removed from his house. The note was apparently a message asking for the removal of his car.

However, his parents kept the car in place in case of Landry’s return.

According to Petito’s family, Brian and Gabby appeared to have a lot of tension. Petito’s mother received an odd text from her a few days before her disappearance.

Netizens took to Twitter to discuss the recent update in the case with most people believing Brian’s involvement in her murder.
