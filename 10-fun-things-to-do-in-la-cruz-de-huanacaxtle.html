10 Fun Things to Do in La Cruz de Huanacaxtle

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, June 23, 2024

Explore the Local Markets

When visiting La Cruz de Huanacaxtle, one of the best ways to experience the local culture is by exploring the vibrant markets. The Sunday market, known as the “Mercado de La Cruz,” offers a wide range of products, from fresh fruits and vegetables to handmade crafts and clothing. Stroll through the colorful stalls, interact with the friendly locals, and indulge in delicious street food. Don’t forget to pick up some authentic Mexican spices, artisanal souvenirs, or unique pieces of jewelry to bring back home with you.

Sail Along the Banderas Bay

La Cruz de Huanacaxtle is nestled on the shores of the beautiful Banderas Bay, making it a perfect destination for sailing enthusiasts. Charter a boat or join a guided tour to explore the crystal-clear waters and witness breathtaking sunsets. Feel the refreshing ocean breeze as you sail past stunning coastal landscapes, secluded beaches, and majestic rock formations. You can also try your hand at fishing or even spot some dolphins and whales along the way.

Indulge in Local Cuisine

No visit to La Cruz de Huanacaxtle is complete without savoring the delectable local cuisine. Head to the waterfront restaurants and sample authentic Mexican dishes, such as shrimp tacos, ceviche, or traditional mole. Don’t miss the opportunity to taste the famous “pescado zarandeado,” a local grilled fish dish marinated in delicious spices. For a unique dining experience, try “street food hopping” and explore the numerous food stalls serving mouthwatering tacos, churros, and fresh coconut water.

Relax on the Beaches

La Cruz de Huanacaxtle boasts several pristine beaches that offer the perfect setting for relaxation and sunbathing. Playa La Manzanilla is a popular spot, known for its calm turquoise waters and soft white sand. Lay back on a beach towel, soak up the sun, and enjoy the peaceful ambiance. If you’re feeling adventurous, you can also try your hand at various water sports, such as paddleboarding, snorkeling, or kayaking.

Visit the Marina Riviera Nayarit

For a taste of luxury and elegance, pay a visit to the Marina Riviera Nayarit. This world-class marina is home to magnificent yachts and sailboats from around the globe. Enjoy a leisurely stroll along the waterfront promenade, admire the stunning vessels, and indulge in upscale shopping and dining experiences. You can also catch a live music performance or simply relax with a refreshing drink while enjoying the picturesque views of the marina.

Explore the Hidden Beaches

Escape the crowds and discover the hidden gems of La Cruz de Huanacaxtle by exploring its secluded beaches. Rent a kayak or take a boat tour to reach these tranquil spots that are often tucked away behind cliffs or accessible only by the sea. Las Islitas, situated just south of the town, is a gorgeous beach known for its calm waters and abundant marine life. Spend a day snorkeling, swimming, or simply basking in the natural beauty of these untouched coastal treasures.

Embark on an Eco-Tour

La Cruz de Huanacaxtle is surrounded by lush jungles and diverse ecosystems, making it an ideal destination for eco-tourism. Join an eco-tour and embark on an adventure through the Sierra Madre mountains, where you can hike through picturesque trails, swim in refreshing waterfalls, or even zip-line through the canopy. Learn about the local flora and fauna from knowledgeable guides and immerse yourself in the natural wonders that this region has to offer.

Experience Traditional Festivals

Immerse yourself in the vibrant culture of La Cruz de Huanacaxtle by experiencing one of its traditional festivals. The town is known for its lively celebrations, where locals and visitors come together to enjoy music, dance, and delicious food. The Festival of the Virgin of Guadalupe in December is a particularly spectacular event, featuring parades, fireworks, and religious processions. Make sure to check the local calendar when planning your trip to catch one of these festive occasions.

Go Fishing in Banderas Bay

With its prime location on Banderas Bay, La Cruz de Huanacaxtle is a haven for fishing enthusiasts. Charter a fishing boat and set off on an exciting deep-sea fishing adventure. Test your skills as you try to catch marlin, sailfish, or dorado. Whether you’re an experienced angler or a novice, the local fishermen will be more than happy to guide you and share their tips and tricks. Reeling in a big catch will undoubtedly be a memorable highlight of your trip.

Discover the Petroglyphs in Alta Vista

Venture into the nearby village of Alta Vista and explore its ancient petroglyphs. These intriguing pre-Hispanic rock carvings offer a glimpse into the rich history and cultural heritage of the region. Hike through the rugged terrain and witness the incredible craftsmanship of the indigenous people who once inhabited these lands. Take a moment to appreciate the spiritual significance of these archaeological treasures and marvel at their intricate designs.

In conclusion, La Cruz de Huanacaxtle offers a plethora of exciting and immersive experiences for all types of travelers. From exploring local markets and indulging in delicious cuisine to sailing along the stunning coastline and discovering hidden beaches, this charming town is sure to captivate your heart and leave you with unforgettable memories. So pack your bags, escape the everyday hustle, and embark on an adventure in this hidden gem of Nayarit, Mexico.
