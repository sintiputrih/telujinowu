Some Reports Suggest Missing Lina Sardar Khil Was Found, Who Are Her Parents?

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, October 3, 2024

Was Lina Sardar Khil Found Alive? There is no information on a 3-year old Afghani immigrant kid who disappeared from a playground near her home in San Antonio.

Lina Sardar Khil’s disappearance has made a buzz around the town of San Antonio. The 3-year-old was playing very near to her home from where she was nowhere to be found again.

During the press conference on Tuesday, the chief of Police Cheif William Mcmanus said the disappearance happened when Lina’s mother left her kid unattended for some time near the family complex.

Photos: Was Lina Sardar Khil Found Alive?

According to the latest news published in CNN, there has been no information Lina Sardar Khil found alive. The authorities are making their search intensive to locate the kid ASAP.

Furthermore, the Kens5 reporter Henry Ramos spoke to the father of Lina Sardar Khil with the support of a translator who seemed to be agitated with loads of questioning from different authorities.

At the same time, holding his tears, he thanked the official authorities, searching to find his kid with seriousness.

The family moved in from Afghanistan two years back due to the insurgencies in their homeland. Lina Sardar Khil’s father, Riaz Sardar Khil, is an unfortunate father who had to flee his homeland to settle down in the USA.

The family lives close to the Afghani community. They hope to find Khil soon as possible in a healthy state.

Who Are Lina Sardar Khil Parents – Riaz Sardar Khil And Mrs Khil?

Lina Sardar Khil parents are Riaz Sardar Khil and Mrs Khil. They are a small family living in San Antonio, expecting a new family member in recent months.

The family believes that Lina was abducted, but the officials now treat the case as disappearance. The case has been widespread and touched the hearts of many people.

Many people have come to help the family from California to New York in this devastating time. The Islamic Center has declared the reward of $75 K on any information on Lina.
