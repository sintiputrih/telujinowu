Meet Stephanie Cozart Burton Photos of LeVar Burtons Wife and Mother of a Daughter

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, July 29, 2024

Stephanie Cozart Burton is famously known for being the wife of actor LeVar Burton. But what many people don’t know is that LeVar Burton’s wife Stephanie Cozart Burton is a talented makeup artist who has also won an Emmy for her work.

The gorgeous Stephanie Cozart and her husband LeVar Burton got married on the 3rd of October 1992. The pair of LeVar and Stephanie has a beautiful daughter named Michaela Jean Burton. Apart from that, Stephanie Cozart Burton’s husband LeVar Burton has a son named Eian Burton from his previous relationship.

LeVar Burton’s Wife Stephanie Cozart Burton, Award Winning Makeup Artist

As stated earlier, many people only know Stephanie as the wife of LeVar Burton but what many people have got no idea about is that Stephanie is also a talented makeup artist. She won a Daytime Emmy Award for Outstanding Makeup in 2013 for ““ an American talk show. Alongside that, she has been nominated for the Award a couple of times.

The funny thing is LeVar Burton and his wife Stephanie Cozart Burton first met one another on the sets of the series “The Roots” where she was doing his makeup. Stephanie has worked her magic in almost all the field, from TVs, movies, editorial, and wedding too. Her first show on TV was “Facts Of Life” where she worked as a second assistant. And then, later on, she was promoted to Department Head, now see where she is.

LeVar Burton’s wife Stephanie has done the makeup of many famous artists including George Clooney, Tracee Ellis Ross, Jenifer Lopez, Jim Carrey, and others. When asked what she loves about makeup, she replied that it is transformative. Her advice for the up-and-coming makeup artists is that they should attend workshops, and do what they like the most.
