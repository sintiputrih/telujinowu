One Punch Man season 3 release window, cast, and more

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, May 5, 2024

One Punch Man first graced TV screens in 2015 and brought with it fun, fighting anime action with a reasonably simple premise. The series follows Saitama, a superhero with the power to defeat his enemies with a single punch.

Recommended Videos

Though some fans were disappointed with the second season, as it failed to live up to the fine-tuned anime perfection of the first, they’re still eagerly awaiting more One Punch Man to continue its story.

When will One Punch Man season 3 air?

Right now, there is no official date for when fans will get a chance to see season three, but the official website for the show has officially confirmed that the new season will be coming. For now, it is expected to land in 2024.

One thing that is for sure, is that One Punch Man fans aren’t strangers to waiting. Between the first two seasons, there was a four-year gap so we may be seeing a similar thing between seasons two and three.

Season three will follow another trend for the anime with MAPPA taking the reins on the series as its third animation studio in as many seasons. Of course, this could be one part of the delay given how busy the studio has been with its many hit shows.

Don’t stress! we will update this article as soon as One Punch Man season three has a release date.

One Punch Man voice cast

Season three is expected to introduce new characters into the story, but of course, we will get many of our favorites making their return. Because of this, we do have an idea of what cast members will be coming back again.

As any anime fan will know, the voice cast differs between its Japanese and English releases. Here is a look at the returning cast of One Punch Man.

  • Saitama – Japanese: Makoto Furukawa, English: Max Mittelman
  • Genos – Japanese: Kaito Ishikawa, English: Zach Aguilar
  • Garou – Japanese: Hikaru Midorikawa, English: Greg Chun
  • Orochi – Japanese: Atsushi Ono, English: Jason Marnocha

Again, this list of characters will be updated once more details are confirmed.
