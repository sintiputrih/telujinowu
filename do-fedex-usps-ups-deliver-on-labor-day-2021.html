Do FedEx, USPS, and UPS deliver on President's Day?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, July 18, 2024

SHOPPERS around the country may be wondering if their online orders and general mail will be delivered on President's Day.

Here's everything you need to know about the annual federal holiday and whether your mail will be delayed.

Do FedEx, USPS, and UPS deliver on Labor Day?

President's Day usually falls on the third Monday in February. This year it will be celebrated on Monday, February 19, 2024.

The United States Postal Service will not be open on President's Day. There will also be no mail delivery due to the holiday.

UPS and FedEx will also be closed, but UPS Express Critical service will remain available.

USPS, UPS, and FedEx all have their 2024 holiday schedules listed online.

A list of fast-food chain restaurants will remain open, including McDonald's and Wendy's.

What is President's Day?

President's Day commemorates the birthday of George Washington, the first President of the United States born on February 22, 1732.

President's Day was first established over a century since Washington was born in 1879.

However, it wasn't until 1968, that the holiday was made a federal holiday after former president Lyndon B. Johnson signed it into law.

The federal holiday was celebrated on the 22nd every year until 1971 when it was officially changed to the third Monday of February.

The day has since been repurposed as a celebration of the birthdays of every president who has served the United States.
