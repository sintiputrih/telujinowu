What's Going On With Their Friendship These Days?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, July 31, 2024

Reality television star and makeup mogul Kylie Jenner stopped being friends with model Jordyn Woods after news broke of Jordyn and Khloe Kardashian's boyfriend Tristan Thompson hooking up in early 2019. Since then, many have been hopeful that the two ladies would manage to get over the scandal and rekindle their friendship.

Today, we're taking a closer look at just what the two have said about each other since then — and where does their relationship stand in 2022? Keep on scrolling to find out!

8 Are Kylie Jenner And Jordyn Woods Friends On Instagram?

As most of those who keep up with the Kardashian/Jenners know, Kylie Jenner cut all ties with Jordyn Woods after news of the cheating scandal broke. A source for People magazine revealed in August 2019 that "she wants to live as drama-free as possible.

Related: The Truth About Jordyn Woods’ Relationship With Her Mom

She just doesn’t need Jordyn in her life. Unfollowing her on Instagram was kind of the last step to break away from Jordyn." As of writting, the two ladies have not re-followed each other on Instagram.

7 Jordyn Woods Opened Up About The Tristan Thompson Incident In 2019

During her appearance on Jada Pinkett Smith's Red Table Talk, Jordyn Woods opened up about what happened between her and Khloe Kardashian's baby daddy. "I was drunk. I was not tipsy, I was drunk. But I was not beyond the point of recollection. I know where I was. But, on the way out [of Tristan's house] he did kiss me. No passion, no nothing. On the way out he just kissed me. It was a kiss on the lips. No tongue kiss, no making out. Nothing. I don't think he's wrong either, because I allowed myself to be in this position," Woods revealed. "I didn't know how to feel. I was like, 'That didn't just happen,' because I was leaving already so I walked out immediately after. And I got in the car and was like, 'No, that didn't happen,' I [told him] I need to go. I was in shock."

6 Kylie Jenner Talked About Jordyn In The 'KUWTK' Finale In 2021

During the Keeping Up With Kardashians reunion which aired in June 2021, Kylie Jenner talked about her feelings toward Jordyn Woods. "Jordyn and I did have a talk after that," the reality television star admitted. "When we were friends, we never thought that we wouldn't be friends. It was kind of an overnight thing, and, you know, when she did something to my family, it felt like she did something to me." Considering that this was over two years after the incident, it's safe to say that the two have not given their friendship another chance. After the reunion aired, fans were not happy with the fact that Kylie is still blaming Jordyn, but not Tristan.

5 Does Khloe Kardashian Stand In The Way Of Kylie And Jordyn's Friendship?

In the same reunion of Keeping Up With Kardashians, Khloe Kardashian clarified that she wouldn't mind if her sister Kylie Jenner rekindled her friendship with Jordyn Woods. "I have told Kylie intimately that I would genuinely not care whatsoever if Kylie wants to be her friend again," Khloe said. "My sisters matter way more to me than any grudge or issue that I would have with another individual. And if I can allow Tristan back into my life, I need to allow the same forgiveness and acceptance of other people."

4 Does Kris Jenner Want Kylie And Jordyn To Be Friends Again?

Two years after the cheating scandal, Kris Jenner sent Jordyn Woods a PR package from Safely — her line of eco-friendly cleaning products with Chrissy Teigen.

Related: The Muddy Truth About Kylie Jenner's Billionaire Status In 2022

In April 2021, Jordyn Woods shared photos of the PR package on her Instagram, and apart from the products she also received a handwritten note and a bouquet of fresh flowers.

3 Jordyn Woods Hopes Her And Kylie Jenner Find Their Way Back To Each Other

Back in the summer of 2019, Jordyn Woods revealed that she wants nothing but the best for her former best friend. "I love her," Woods said. "That’s my homie. I hope everything falls into place and that we can all grow and build our relationships with our family and God and come back together one day and be stronger and happier."

2 Do Kylie Jenner And Jordyn Woods Run Into Each Other?

Anyone who keeps up with the two stars knows that even though Kylie and Jordyn aren't close friends anymore, the two still run in the same circles and have plenty of mutual friends. According to Life & Style, some of their mutual friends include Megan Thee Stallion, Anastasia Karanikolaou, Sofia Richie, Hailey Baldwin, and Jaden Smith. In fact, according to the outlet, Jordyn Woods might have been present when Kylie Jenner hung out with Megan Thee Stallion in July 2020.

1 Kylie And Jordyn Still Have Photos Together On Instagram

While many may have expected that the two former besties would delete the photos in which they are together, it seems as if both of them kept the pics on their feed. It is impossible to tell if there is a reason for this, but many fans believe that they kept the photos because they miss each other, and they believe that one day they will manage to be close again.
