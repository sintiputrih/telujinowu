Kim Kardashian's Rick Owens Dress and Balenciaga Shoes: Look for Less

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, September 6, 2024

Mommy swag!

For Kim Kardashian, dressing like a mom means throwing on a high fashion dress and accessorizing with the most coveted heels of the season. 

The Keeping Up With the Kardashians star alerted her Instagram followers on Wednesday, Sept. 10 to her favorite shoe of the moment: Balenciaga’s d’Orsay pumps, which cost $895.  

The sartorial moment she posted also featured a fitted black turtleneck dress for $591 by Rick Owens, and black sunglasses by Celine.  

Though we’re sure a luxury outfit costing almost $2,000 was worth the splurge for the reality star, it is our duty at Us Weekly to help you nab a bargain. 

Norma Kamali’s budget-friendly line Kamali Kulture has a similar long sleeve turtleneck dress for $98.00.
