Who is Yolanda Adams? | Yolanda Adams Biography and Wiki

 Yolanda Yvette Adams is an American gospel singer, actress, record producer, and former radio host of her own nationally syndicated morning gospel show. She was born on August 27, 1961, in Houston, Texas. She is the eldest in a family of six children.

Yolanda was discovered by producer/keyboardist Ben Tankard, and signed to his independent music label, Tribute Records and launched Through The Storm. Over the course of her career, Yolanda has sold nearly 10 million albums globally according to SoundScan.

Adams is referred to as the “Queen of Contemporary Gospel Music” as well as the “First Lady of Modern Gospel”. Variety dubbed Adams as the “Reigning Queen of Urban Gospel”. On December 11, 2009, Billboard named Adams the No. 1 Gospel Artist of the last decade In the same chart, her album Mountain High…Valley Low was recognized as the best gospel album.

Yolanda Adams Gospel Singer

Yolanda first landed in the entertainment industry working as a part-time model and school teacher in Houston. She then transitioned to working as a full-time lead singer. Through her powerful and gifted voice, Yolanda quickly caught the attention of great producers who wanted to work with her.  Amongst the people that noticed this woman’s gift, is Thomas Whitfield, who signed her to Sound of Gospel Records as a lead singer with Houston’s Southeast Inspirational Choir.

In 1982, the choir launched the single, For My Liberty with her as the featured vocalist. She also appeared in a music video Give Us Peace in 1986. In the following years, she inked a recording deal with Sound of Gospel that helped her release her first album Just As I Am in 1987.

Over the years, she has dominated the world of contemporary gospel music and has also managed to earn the hearts of lots of fans across the world. Today, her name has become a household name. Yolanda Adams has also released several music videos like The Battle Is The Lord’s, More Than a Melody, Gotta Have Love, Songs From The Heart and many more, some of which are mentioned at the bottom of this post.

Yolanda Adams’s Christmas album released in 2000 and a live album (The Experience) in 2001. She has also lent her voice in the 2003 film entitled I Believe. Moreover, she got her first book Points of Power published in 2010.

Yolanda Adams Radio Show

Yolanda has a radio show called, The Yolanda Adams Morning Show. It aired in over 26 radio markets for about two years. Her radio show featured Marcus D. Wiley as Bishop Secular. The show aired for a decade before ending in 2016. Yolanda Adams show relaunched in September 2019 from Texas Southern University’s KTSU-FM 90.9.
